Detant workshops for non-practitioners

The DETANT workshops create a safe learning environment that teaches emotional regulation and power in stressful times. These practices and insights are the result of our experience in two diametrical fields - the insights gained from teaching martial arts in AKBAN and the Israeli security situation. The procedures in our workshops give an exact set of tools. Using these techniques, the cognitive, the emotional and the physical cross-influence, but it is mainly through the physical - and that means done in body - practices that we can set a measured reaction, stay focused and maintain control in emotionally chaotic times.
