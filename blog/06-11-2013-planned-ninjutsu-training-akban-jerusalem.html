There are several ways to apply forward force on an opponent. The most simple is a two handed, simultaneous, push with a tsugi ashi. This Wednesday we will repeat this push and move on to diversify Uke's push technique an a way that will allow for flow in high energy scenarios. On this intricate execution phase we will be dealing with the low level of violence. The sessions start with Ninjutsu kamae followed by the himum. The session ends with Randori.
