Ten minutes after the rocket launch on Tel Aviv - AKBAN as usual

Ten minutes after clearing the dojo because of the alarm that precedes the rockets that were fired toward Tel Aviv. We were training on Shoshin Gata from the techniques of Gyokko ryu. We just resumed training. To all the spouses, we have a 'state of art' bomb shelter on the grounds.
15/11/2012
AKBAN Journal, Security, Author: Yossi Sheriff
