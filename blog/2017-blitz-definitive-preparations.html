2017 Blitz, definitive preparations

Every August according to tradition in AKBAN we conduct sparring for most of the duration of the practice sessions. This is one tough month that necessitates top fitness, technique and ego control. This week we will conclude a great training cycle in Tel Aviv, Jerusalem and Ramat Hasharon dojo and in Tuesday will start the 2017 Blitz. Godspeed!
