Planned Ninjutsu training, AKBAN Ramat Hasharon - 30.9.2013

This Monday we are exploring the opposite direction of sequence number 4. Instead of checking the limbs to close distance into one of the 5 basic themes, we move out side the opponent's grip using daken and kansetsu waza and using medium level of violence. The session will start with Ninjutsu kamae followed by the himum. We finish the session with full contact Randori. A video summery of this session will be uploaded to Ninjutsu training in video category in the AKBAN wiki.
