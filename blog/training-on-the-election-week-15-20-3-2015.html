Training on the election week, 15-20.3.2015

There will not be a "24" event this week. Also, because of the election, there will not be a session on Tuesday, on election day. At Thursday we will have an extended session in Tel Aviv dojo. At Friday we are going to have a ECR session in Tel Aviv. In all the groups we will be working on Takagi and Fudo Kata to prepare for the Sixth Colloquium.
