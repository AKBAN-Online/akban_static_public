Weekly Ninjutsu plan for Tel Aviv, Jerusalem and Ramat Hasharon dojo, 5.1.2014-9.1.2014

This Tuesday in Tel Aviv we will be at the temporary dojo at 193 Ben Yehuda St unless we'll get the papers from the Tel Aviv municipality. At all the other dojos we'll be training as usual. For questions, please call me at:052-5108747 The sessions start with Ninjutsu kamae followed by the himum. The session ends with Randori.
