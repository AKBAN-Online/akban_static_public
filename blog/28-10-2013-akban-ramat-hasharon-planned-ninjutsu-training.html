28.10.2013, AKBAN Ramat Hasharon, Planned Ninjutsu training

The relevant techniques change not only according to the opponent, but in conjunction to the many variables a real combat presents. This is why many times the weakest technique, the lower level of violence, is the best tactics. This Monday we will continue our effort from last week's training and expand from the Ganseki throw we worked into other High and Medium level of violence techniques. The session will start with Ninjutsu kamae followed by the himum. We finish the session with full contact Randori. A video summery of this session will be uploaded to Ninjutsu training in video category in the AKBAN wiki.
