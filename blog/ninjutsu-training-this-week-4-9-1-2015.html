Ninjutsu training this week, 4-9.1.2015

This Friday we are having ECR group in Tel Aviv. In the dojos we will continue working on integrating sequence number 4 (Kansetsu Waza Junjo) of variations 1,2,4 into more challenging work of Daken Taijutsu. Please do not forget punching gloves, we are upping the intensity. At the Combat Kata Groups we will learn Yama Otoshi and Tobi CHigai no kata and combine them with sequence 4, variations 1,2. We are having a Bor Sud at Thursday night. If you worked in a film crew and can dedicate a night, let me know.
