Planned training 19.6.2013 - AKBAN Jerusalem dojo

At both sessions we will work with static armlocks from standing position. We will work with kicks to pressure points and combine these with the combos we have been learning last weeks. On the second, veterans' session we will also analyse a Fudo ryu kata. The sessions start with Ninjutsu kamae followed by the himum. The session ends with Randori.
