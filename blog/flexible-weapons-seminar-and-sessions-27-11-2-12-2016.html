Flexible weapons seminar and sessions, 27.11-2.12.2016

This Thursday, 1.12.2016 from 18:00-21:00 we will have a National seminar on Flexible weapons. The seminar will take place in AKBAN Honbu dojo Tel Aviv and will translate the last week's sessions into homework drills and add even more techniques to the syllabus. During the week we will start working on Fudo Ryu Kata.
