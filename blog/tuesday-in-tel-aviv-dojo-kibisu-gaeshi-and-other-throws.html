Tuesday in Tel Aviv dojo - Kibisu gaeshi and other throws

We will start at the first session with one kata against a straight knife thrust. The session will continue with applying Kibisu gaeshi and striking combos against an opponent's front jab. Second, veteran session, will start with advanced empty hand against knife and continue to various hand throws against a fudo ken.
