Mind reading devices taking control of video games

Last month Emotive announced another device that's claiming to bridge the gap between thoughts and video game controllers. The device reads EEG and transforms them into electrical signal to be used in a variety of systems. This is not the first device that takes the gamer one step closer into the realm where virtual fighting can simulate reality. We reported on the Wii controller earlier this year. I am not too happy about these tech development as I do not think anything can take the place of the Dojo. But I also know for sure - nothing will stop technology. Is this the beginning of the end for martial arts? We have already seen flight drones replacing real pilots. Are UFC fighters the obvious next step?
