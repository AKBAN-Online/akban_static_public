14.10.2013, AKBAN Ramat Hasharon, Planned Ninjutsu training

This Monday we will explore some intricate counter clinch techniques stemming from Fudo ryu and Kukishin Ryu. The second half of the session will deal with honing three kicks and combining these with foot work into combos. The session will start with Ninjutsu kamae followed by the himum. We finish the session with full contact Randori. A video summery of this session will be uploaded to Ninjutsu training in video category in the AKBAN wiki.
