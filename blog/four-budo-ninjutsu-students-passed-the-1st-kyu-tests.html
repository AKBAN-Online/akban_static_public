Four Budo Ninjutsu students passed the 1st kyu tests

Four students passed the brown belt tests with flying colors. These tests necessitate more then 6-7 years of dojo training and are a preliminary to the long time it takes (13 years)to collect enough experience for the 1st Dan (black belt) in dojos that graduated the Akban school. Three veterans oversaw the tests that included all of the 1st kyu material in the test book. Link to the Budo Ninjutsu grade requirements with videos of the techniques. Not all schools need such a long time to prepare the students. We do and we still have mixed feelings about it.
15/04/2008
AKBAN Journal, Belt exams, Author: Yossi Sheriff
