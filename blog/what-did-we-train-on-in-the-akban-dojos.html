What did we train on in the AKBAN dojos?

In Beit Hakerem Dojo we started with sakanagare no kata and emphasized Kata Guruma. At the German colony Dojo we continued with Daken and punching techniques in addition to several wrestling throws. At the Tedi, Ramat Hasharon and Tel Aviv Dojos we dealt with sacrifice throws Tai Sabaki and Hissaku no kata
