Training times during the Sukot Holiday

The martial art groups of the Jerusalem Tedi dojo will be doing Budo Ninjutsu during The Sukot Holiday on Sunday, September the 30th in Gan Havradim at regular training hours. The Ramat Hasharon and Tel Aviv Dojo will have a joined training session on Tuesday, October the 2nd.
