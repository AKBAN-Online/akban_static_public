First week of the AKBAN Blitz, 31.7-4.8.2016

The Blitz month opens this week. The Jerusalem groups spar on Sundays and Wednesdays, the Tel Aviv and Ramat hasharon groups spar at Tuesdays and Thursdays. All groups can come to sessions on different dojos. The randori is videoed, edited into slow-motion and sent, accompanied by a Debriefing form, to your personal whatsapp or mailbox.
