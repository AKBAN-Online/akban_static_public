Weekend quote - Jōshū (Zhaozhou Congshen)

"If the right man preaches the wrong way," said Joshu, "the way will follow the man and become right. If the wrong man preaches the right way, the way will follow the man and become wrong." From Radical Zen: The sayings of Joshu, Translated by Yoel Hofman
30/05/2014
AKBAN Journal, Author: Yossi Sheriff, Quotation
