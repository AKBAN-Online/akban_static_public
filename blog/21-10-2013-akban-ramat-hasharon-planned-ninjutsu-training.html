21.10.2013, AKBAN Ramat Hasharon, Planned Ninjutsu training

This Monday we will work with techniques of Koto Ryu and Shinden Fudo Ryu Dakentaijutsu emphasizing Sabaki and Moguri. Using Tangeki no kata and a Ki Gata of Bobi no kamae, we will use its Daken to create Kuzushi for both Ganseki Nage and Muso dori. The session concludes with applying Yokuto Kata tactics to fast sparring combos.
The session will start with Ninjutsu kamae followed by the himum. We finish the session with full contact Randori. A video summery of this session will be uploaded to Ninjutsu training in video category in the AKBAN wiki.
