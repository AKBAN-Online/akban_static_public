10.11.2013 Planned Ninjutsu session - AKBAN Jerusalem

This Sunday we are continuing the Uke work we have been doing over the last week. Emphasizing correct push and coherence we will use this technique to work Gyokko Ryu kata. The sessions start with Ninjutsu kamae followed by the himum. The session ends with Randori. We upload selected Ninjutsu lessons to the Ninjutsu training category at the AKBAN wiki.
