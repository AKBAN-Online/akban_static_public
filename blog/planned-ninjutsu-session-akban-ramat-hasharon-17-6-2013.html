Planned Ninjutsu session, AKBAN Ramat Hasharon - 17.6.2013

We will go over kicks that we rarely used this semester and combine them with the advanced combos we practiced last weeks. The second half of the session will be devoted to sitting techniques and their application in self defence situations. The session will start with Ninjutsu kamae followed by the himum. We finish the session with full contact Randori. A video summery of this session will be uploaded to Ninjutsu training in video category in the AKBAN wiki.
