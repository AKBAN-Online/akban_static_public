Sukkot intensive dakentaijutsu seminar - AKBAN 2013

AKBAN academy veterans, Elad Nagar and Ra'am Manzur will lead this Sukkot holiday the advanced pragmatic base of hitting in Ninjutsu (Dakentaijutsu). In three afternoons we will improve accuracy, power and explosive speed of our punches and kicks. For this intensive and demanding seminar practitioners need: punching gloves, protection gear and water. The seminar is open to AKBAN Tel Aviv, Jerusalem and Ramat Hasharon students only.
