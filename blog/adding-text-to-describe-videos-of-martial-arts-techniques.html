Adding text to describe videos of martial arts techniques

We are continuing the second stage in our PROJECT and starting to describe in text the videos of the Martial arts, Ninjutsu - Budo Taijutsu moves and techniques in the AKBAN-wiki. Few veterans started writing text to the visuals. This is an ongoing process and more layers of meaning will probably added in time to many martial art styles. Here are the efforts of Ittai Ariel Daniel and Yossi Registering and adding insights and knowledge to this vast database is free and open.
