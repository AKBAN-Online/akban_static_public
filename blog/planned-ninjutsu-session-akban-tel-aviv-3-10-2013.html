Planned Ninjutsu session, AKBAN Tel Aviv - 3.10.2013

A reminder, we are training at our temporary dojo, 193 Ben Yehuda st. 1st floor. This Thursday we will be integrating the interplay between armlocks and throws in Ninjutsu and focusing on the difficult over-under wrestling clinch. We will start with regular Gi grip and explore Muso dori, Musha dori and the Osoto gake throw. As usual, please bring punching gloves for kata training. The sessions will start with kamae followed by the himum. The session ends with Randori. I upload selected Ninjutsu lessons to the Ninjutsu training category at the AKBAN wiki.
