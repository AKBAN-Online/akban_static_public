AKBAN seminars in Sukkot, 27.9-1.10.2015

We are emphasizing traditional weapons at the start of this training year. During the Sukkot holiday we will continue to practice sword, short stick and knife techniques. The seminars are constructed so that trainees have a stratified routine to take home and practice with. The last part, on Friday, will be videoed for future reference. Please use Waze app in Friday because of the Tel Aviv Bicycle race. We start all training sessions earlier, so calculate times accordingly.

Seminar schedules

  • Tel Aviv, Tuesday, 29 September, From 17:30 till 20:30 - Event 1 on Facebook
  • Jerusalem, Wednesday, 30 September, From 17:00 till 20:00 - Event Jerusalem on Facebook
  • Tel Aviv, Thursday, 1 October, From 18:00 till 20:00 - Event 2 on Facebook
  • Tel Aviv, Friday, 2 October, From 08:00 till 10:00 - Event 3 on Facebook
With the dojo-cho permission, all seminars are open to all AKBAN students.
