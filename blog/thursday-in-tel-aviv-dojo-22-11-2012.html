Thursday in Tel Aviv dojo - 22.11.2012

Mind you, We have a bomb shelter at the Tel Aviv dojo. From now on, the entrance to AKBAN Tel Aviv is from the cafeteria door. The Parking lot iron door will be closed. Starting, as usual, from the Ki and moto gata derived from the Ninjutsu combat stances, we will then do the Himum. At the session we will work the inside range of the opponent's attack using Fudo Ryu and Kukishinden ryu kata. I am intensifying the documentation project especially for our veterans who can not be present because of the security situation in Israel. The video of this Ninjutsu session will be online at the Ninjutsu at war time section at Friday. Stay safe.
