Planned session for AKBAN Tel Aviv - 2.7.2013

Combo July starts! In this Tuesday session, on both the first and second sessions, we will work with gruelling set of combos to hone and prepare for the oncomming August Blitz. We are starting to give each combo a mnemonic, and I embedded a youtube that explains the process (AKA Roger Mayweather method) in Western Boxing. All the combos we will learn this summer will be linked to their relevant Ninjutsu katas. The sessions will conclude with Uchi komi of Hip throws. A reminder: Our dojo has moved up for the summer. We will be training at the third floor of the school's building because of the renovations of our dojo. The sessions will start with kamae followed by the himum. The session ends with Randori.
