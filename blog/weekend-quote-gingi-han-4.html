Weekend quote - Gingi Han

Signs that warn: "beware of pickpockets" are a favorite hangout of pickpockets. They base their felony on fear derived attention. It's the best place to know where valuables are. People will always touch the place when they see the sign. When one looks deeper one sees different reality. Taken with permission from Wasifat Salata Min Binat Gishrit By Gingi Han
