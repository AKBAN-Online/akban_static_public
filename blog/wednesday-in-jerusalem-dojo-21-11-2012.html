Wednesday in Jerusalem dojo - 21.11.2012

The Jerusalem Ninjutsu dojo is equipped with a bomb shelter. We start today, as usual, with the moto gata from Ninjutsu kamae. After doing the fitness routine, the Himum, we will continue with Tai sabaki combined with punching and kicking drills. Today we will go over Ninjutsu kicks combined with sabaki drills. At the second session we will work with Kukishinden Ryu kata. The video from this sessions will be online on the Ninjutsu at wartime link the day after.
