Ninjutsu training this week, 1-6.2.2015

This Friday, 6.6.2015, we will have Edge combat research group in honbu dojo. We are starting the preparation for Amir Haimi Sensei (Alan Moraes BJJ Black Belt, AKBAN Sandan) that will happen in two weeks. We will use Togakure Ryu ground sabaki as a physical preparation. Here's a link to event page on Facebook. In all the groups we will hone Nage waza approaches against clinch from two perspectives - the Kukishinden Ryu and the Fudo Ryu perspective. In the Combat Kata groups we will try to finish learning 3 remaining kata from the Shirabe Moguri gata level.
