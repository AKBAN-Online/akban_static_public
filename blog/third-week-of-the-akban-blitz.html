Third week of the AKBAN Blitz

The sparring we do during the Blitz month are recorded in slo-mo video, edited and are sent to private WhatsApp accounts of every AKBAN practitioner. Every bout is debriefed, using the Methodical pyramid, and the conclusions are applied in the next session. Every week I video, edit and send more then 150 bouts. The "120" concluding event will take place in Wednesday, August the 30th, 18:00 in Tel Aviv. I will update on the exact place.
