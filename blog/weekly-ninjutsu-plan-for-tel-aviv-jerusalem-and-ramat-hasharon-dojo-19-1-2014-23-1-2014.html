Weekly Ninjutsu plan for Tel Aviv, Jerusalem and Ramat Hasharon dojo, 19.1.2014 - 23.1.2014

19 January update: We are returning to our regular dojo. 294 Hayarkon St. At all the other dojos we'll be training as usual. We will be using the 4.1 combo to enter Kansetsu waza and Nage waza timed with the fast sabaki step we have been learning lately. Please do not forget to bring punching gloves as we always validate our Ninjutsu with randori. For questions, please call me at:052-5108747 The sessions start with Ninjutsu kamae followed by the himum. The session ends with Randori.
