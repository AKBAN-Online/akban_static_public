Planned session, Ramat Hasharon dojo - 27.5.2013

We will work on ganseki throw and closing the distance to this position from striking distance. We will combine this work with an advanced session on Uke work and ukemi. The session will start with Ninjutsu kamae followed by the himum. We finish the session with full contact Randori. A video summery of this session will be uploaded to Ninjutsu training in video category in the AKBAN wiki.
