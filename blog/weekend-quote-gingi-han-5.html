Weekend quote - Gingi Han

These are the roots of the teacher's work: Kata, Randori and Mondo. Kata is the seal of order. Randori is the meeting place of chaos and order. Mondo is needed to explain to the thinking part what is happening in the doing part. These roots must be watered by constant respect, respect for others, respect for the insights and respect for one's own constituents.
