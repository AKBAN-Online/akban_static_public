Weekend quote - G. E. Moore

"In ordinary speech, I want this, I like this, I care about this, are constantly used as equivalents for I think this good. And in this way it is very natural to be led to suppose that there is no distinct class of ethical judgments, but only the class things enjoyed; Principia Ethica (1903) by G. E. Moore
