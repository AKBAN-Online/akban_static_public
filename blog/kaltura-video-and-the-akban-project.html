Kaltura video and the Akban project

Kaltura, the new guys on the web video applications, recently partnered with the Wikimedia Foundation to provide collaborative video functionality to Wikipedia's 207 Million unique visitors. We are really glad they picked our project - The Akban-wiki martial arts encyclopedia - as a featured costumer and partner. Kaltura lets users edit video online from a variety of sources to create informative new videos from existing ones. This correlates perfectly with our community wiki because Kaltura allows wiki editing and history of user's edits. We have been integrating and working out minor early bugs in the Kaltura application and we can present some modest results.
12/03/2008
