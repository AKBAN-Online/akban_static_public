Planned syllabus for Jerusalem dojo - 23.1.2013

At the first session we will repeat the basic striking combos. At the second session we will start learning Kata from the Ten No kata level of Shinden Fudo ryu Dakentaijutsu and repeat some of the recently learned throws. The session starts, as always with Ninjutsu kamae followed by the himum, and ends with Randori. A video summery of this session will be uploaded to Ninjutsu training in video category in the AKBAN wiki.
