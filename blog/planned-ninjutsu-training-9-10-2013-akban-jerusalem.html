Planned Ninjutsu training 9.10.2013 - AKBAN Jerusalem

This Wednesday, at the first session, we will learn new Ninjutsu throws and analyse the moto gata of some of the Ninjutsu stances we train with. At the second, veteran's session, we will continue with the techniques against a wrestling clinch repeating and relearning the transferees into half clinch, over under and full body clinches. The sessions start with Ninjutsu kamae followed by the himum. The session ends with Randori. I upload selected Ninjutsu lessons to the Ninjutsu training category at the AKBAN wiki.
