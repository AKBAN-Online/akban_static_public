Ninjutsu sessions this week, 1-5.6.2014

Sunday at the Jerusalem dojo and Monday at Ramat Hasharon dojo will go as usual. We will continue our pursuit for more opportunities of shoulder locks in Randori. We will not train during Tuesday and Wednesday because of the Shavuot Holiday. At Thursday session in Tel Aviv we will briefly go over Oni Kudaki derivatives and start to build the 5th, Sabaki sequence. There will not be any weapon training this Friday because of place constraints.
