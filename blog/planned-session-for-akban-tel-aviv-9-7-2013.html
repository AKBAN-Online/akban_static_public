Planned session for AKBAN Tel Aviv - 9.7.2013

A reminder: Our dojo has moved up for the summer. We will be training at the third floor of the school's building because of the renovations of our dojo. After getting acquainted with the methodical build of the unique combos we have been learning, we will do two things, the first one - integrating more possibilities to these combos, and the second one is learning the beginning of a third combo and the principles that govern its build. These combos encapsulate the sabaki insights, timings and daken of many Ninjutsu kata. This July we will learn five of these sequences. The sessions will start with kamae followed by the himum. The session ends with Randori. I upload selected Ninjutsu lessons to the Ninjutsu training category at the AKBAN wiki. Tel Aviv Martial arts academy - AKBAN Call us: 052-5108747
