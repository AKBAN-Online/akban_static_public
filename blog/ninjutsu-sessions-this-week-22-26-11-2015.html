Ninjutsu sessions this week, 22-26.11.2015

This week we will not have an ECR session due to AKBAN seminar in Athens, Greece. During the week we will work in two separate areas. We will continue to practice the Ninjutsu counters to Muai Tai clinch and we will start exploring Muto Dori, in preparation for the 7th international colloquium in January. The Muto Dori kata in the Takamatzu Den Ryu are based in deep integration of Tai Sabaki and, of course, realistic execution of weapon attacks.
