Ninjutsu sessions this week, 20-25.12.2015

This Friday, 25.12.2015, 08:00 sharp, we have an ECR session in Tel Aviv dojo. At Honbu dojo, Tel Aviv, we will continue to practice combat formations of two different realms: Entering to trapping to grappling and weapons against Muto dori. We will also do some additional work connecting Kamae, Mudra and Prana Udana.
