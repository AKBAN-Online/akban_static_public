Six more Koto ryu katas added to the Akban-wiki

We filmed and uploaded six more martial arts katas from the koryu school of Koto ryu. This leaves only about 15 missing katas to finish the first stage of documenting this ancient martial school. The Akban project aims to document and classify many martial arts in a free martial arts encyclopedia. New Koto ryu kata in video: 1. Kakuhi no kata 2. Koto no kata, Okuden level 3. Kyogi no kata 4. Santo no kata 5. Shisen no kata 6. Suito no kata Koto ryu article in the Akban-wiki.
