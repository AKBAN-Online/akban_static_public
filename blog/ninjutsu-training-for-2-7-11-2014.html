Ninjutsu training for 2-7.11.2014

This Friday the weapon group will work on Kukishinden middle length stick kata. In the intermediate groups in Jerusalem we are continuing our work with kicks as means for Kansetsu waza. We will work with Hanbo in the Wednesday session and also Gyokko Ryu kata. The Tel Aviv intermediate group will work on kicks and add Muto dori of Gyokko ryu. The advanced, kata, groups in Ramat Hasharon, Tel Aviv and Jerusalem will repeat Gyaku Nage, Uchi Harai and learn a new kata from the Shirabe Moguri level of Kukishinden Ryu.
