Weekend quote - Richard H. Cox

The lower (the) level of cognitive state anxiety, the better the athlete will perform. The notion that a “little” anxiety is good for the athlete is not true if it is of the cognitive variety. From: Richard H. Cox, Sport psychology: concepts and applications, Wm. C. Brown, 1990
