This week in the dojo, 6-11.3.2016

This Friday, 11.3.2016, at 08:00 sharp, in Tel Aviv dojo, we will conduct a Ninjutsu throws seminar with Shmulik Bar Kana Sensei. This seminar is the first in a series dealing with Nage waza in street and randori situations. Here's a link to the event in Facebook. This week in the dojo we will continue our exploration into joint locks and the Ukemi that is particular to Togakure ryu. The Togakure ryu ukemi and Karuwaza deal with new possibilities in movement and spatial orientation. Please bring your punching gloves for the atemi waza.
