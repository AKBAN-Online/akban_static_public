The August blitz - sparring in Akban

In the hottest month in Israel we quadruple the sparring times. This tradition, called "the Blitz", is with us since 1986. It has helped us hone our skills and apply Ninjutsu kata in traditional MMA sparring.

We prepare for this month using our fitness routine and generally having fun.
