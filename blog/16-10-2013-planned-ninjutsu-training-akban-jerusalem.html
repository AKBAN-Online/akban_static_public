16.10.2013, Planned Ninjutsu training - AKBAN Jerusalem

This Wednesday, at the Jerusalem dojo, we will scan the kata from Kurai dori, part of the Koto ryu syllabus. We will see the change in tactics according to opening stance and also its effect on breathing and readiness. In both session we will repeat the techniques against a wrestling clinch and proceed to do some kick sequences.
The sessions start with Ninjutsu kamae followed by the himum. The session ends with Randori. I upload selected Ninjutsu lessons to the Ninjutsu training category at the AKBAN wiki.
