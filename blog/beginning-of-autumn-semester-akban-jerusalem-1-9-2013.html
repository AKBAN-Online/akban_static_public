Beginning of autumn semester, AKBAN Jerusalem - 1.9.2013

This Sunday we will be training at our regular dojo, back at the Tedi stadium center. We will start with two leg throws, move on to relevant short range kicks as kuzushi and combine the two with the fourth combo, 2nd bifurcation. The session will conclude with combos integrating the 4 kicks we learned lately. The sessions start with Ninjutsu kamae followed by the himum. The session ends with Randori.
