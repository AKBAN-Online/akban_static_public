23.10.2013, Planned Ninjutsu training - AKBAN Jerusalem

Since we have been dealing with the intricacies of levels of violence in Ninjutsu, We'll use the first half of both sessions to reevaluate these reactions for different conflict scenarios. We will compare Kata from the intermediate levels of Kukishinden Ryu and use Fudo ryu Dakentaijutsu kata to train with kamae affects on speed and dynamics.
The sessions start with Ninjutsu kamae followed by the himum. The session ends with Randori. I upload selected Ninjutsu lessons to the Ninjutsu training category at the AKBAN wiki. Jerusalem Martial arts academy - AKBAN Phone:052-5108747
