Beginning of autumn semester, AKBAN Ramat Hasharon - 2.9.2013

We are starting the training year in Ramat Hasharon dojo this Monday. We are diving immediately into intricate bifurcations of the grappling combo and uses of these bifurcations in close combat, low level of violence confrontations. The session will start with Ninjutsu kamae followed by the himum. We finish the session with full contact Randori. A video summery of this session will be uploaded to Ninjutsu training in video category in the AKBAN wiki.
