Weekend Quote - H.H. The Dalai Lama

Never give up No matter what is going on Never give up Develop the heart. Too much energy in your country is spent Developing the mind instead of the heart. Be compassionate not just to your friends but to everyone Be compassionate. Work for peace in your heart and in the world. Work for peace and I say again Never give up.
