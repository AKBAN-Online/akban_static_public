Today in Jerusalem dojo - working within the opponent's strikes

We are continuing the work we have been doing inside the striking range of the opponent and between his potential hooks. Moving from side to side using Jodan Yuke we will interchange Osoto gake with Harai goshi (Kukishin ryu) to meet the attacks in the inside range. This is a continuation of the 'two on one' perpective we are currently honing.
