15.10.2013 - planned Ninjutsu session, AKBAN Tel Aviv

A reminder, we are training at our temporary dojo, 193 Ben Yehuda st. 1st floor. This Tuesday we will be concentrating on the the tactics and techniques of various distancing methods in our art. After working with the 10 directions Tai sabaki, we will add Togakure rye perspectives and integrate these with the 5 bifurcations of AKBAN sequence No. 2. The sessions conclude with the counter clinch techniques we trained with last week.
The sessions will start with kamae followed by the himum. The session ends with Randori. I upload selected Ninjutsu lessons to the Ninjutsu training category at the AKBAN wiki. Tel Aviv Martial arts academy - AKBAN Call us: 052-5108747
