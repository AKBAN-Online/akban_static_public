Sunday in Jerusalem dojo - 25.11.2012

We start today, as usual, with the moto gata from Ninjutsu kamae. After doing the fitness routine, the Himum, we will continue with a primer for empty hands against weapon attack. This area of fighting is called Muto dori in Ninjutsu and we will use a Kukishinden ryu kata named Moguri Dori to start exploring the basic sabaki that we use against weapon attack. The techniques of this session, will be uploaded to 25th of November page the day after.
