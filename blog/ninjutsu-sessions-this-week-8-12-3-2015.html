Ninjutsu sessions this week, 8-12.3.2015

This week we will not have a session on Friday. In the dojo we will expand our practice of Nagare (流れ) and will do it through moving the Uke in several axes. Thus, we are preparing the background for the 6th international colloquium in May the 1st. In the COmbat Kata groups we will combine rhythm changes of the Uchikomi and Daken with the Nagare and see how these show in Fudo and Takagi kata. Please bring punching gloves even for the Nagare sessions.
