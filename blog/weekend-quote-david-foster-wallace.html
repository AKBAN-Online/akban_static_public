Weekend quote - David Foster Wallace

Because here's something else that's true. In the day-to-day trenches of adult life, there is actually no such thing as atheism. There is no such thing as not worshipping. Everybody worships. The only choice we get is what to worship. From the 2005 Kenyon Commencement Address.
