Planned session for AKBAN Tel Aviv - 25.6.2013

Our dojo has moved up for the summer. We will be training at the third floor of the school's building because of the renovations of our dojo. We are using chikusei kata to learn inside Jodan Yuke distancing and kuzushi. In the both sessions we will repeat Kusari fundo techniques and lengthen the time we do fitness combos. At the second, veterans' session we will quickly go over the material from the first session and then we will work with one kata and perform kata analysis. The sessions will start with kamae followed by the himum. The session ends with Randori. I upload selected Ninjutsu lessons to the Ninjutsu training category at the AKBAN wiki.
