1st Weapon Seminar - 2010

Short stick, the ultimate self defense weapon, has an extensive kata syllabus in Akban. Together with sparring methodology from Jogo de pau we explore the possibilities of this weapon's low grade of violence. Short chain, Kusarifundo, knife and kata from TSKSR will be learned in the second and third parts of this concise seminar, the first in turn for 2010.
