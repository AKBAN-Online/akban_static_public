Planned Ninjutsu session, AKBAN Ramat Hasharon - 8.7.2013

During this Monday training we will go over our research into the first three Ninjutsu combos. We will check different parameters to prepare for the 2013 August blitz. Before the randori we will do uchi komi of kani basami and tani otoshi. The session will start with Ninjutsu kamae followed by the himum. We finish the session with full contact Randori. A video summery of this session will be uploaded to Ninjutsu training in video category in the AKBAN wiki.
