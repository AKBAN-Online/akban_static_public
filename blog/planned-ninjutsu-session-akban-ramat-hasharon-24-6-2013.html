Planned Ninjutsu session, AKBAN Ramat Hasharon - 24.6.2013

Using the kicks we trained with on our last session we will open up opportunities for throws and arm locks. We will also use the opponent's hand to control daken and apply kuzushi. The session will start with Ninjutsu kamae followed by the himum. We finish the session with full contact Randori. A video summery of this session will be uploaded to Ninjutsu training in video category in the AKBAN wiki.
