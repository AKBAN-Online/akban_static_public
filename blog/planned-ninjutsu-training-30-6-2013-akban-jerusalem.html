Planned ninjutsu training, 30.6.2013 - AKBAN Jerusalem

Today is the last dojo hall meeting before we move on to our regular summer outside dojo. At the training session today we will work with uchi komi of hand throws (te waza) and also take a deeper look at the timings and possibilities of Chikusei kata. The session will conclude with combos integrating the 4 kicks we learned lately. The sessions start with Ninjutsu kamae followed by the himum. The session ends with Randori.
