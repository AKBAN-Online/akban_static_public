Weekend quote - Andy Baio

A modern update to a classic confidence game—find a risky scenario with limited possibilities, bet on every single combination, and then hide your failures. The result is that you look like you’re either psychic or a goddamned genius. Or Never trust a prediction revealed after its outcome. From “How to Flawlessly Predict Anything on the Internet” by @waxpancake
