Tuesday in Tel Aviv dojo - Ukemi waza and improving timing

We start at the 18:00 session with Ukemi and Kaiten waza against Ura gyaku and Oni Kudaki. Using yoko sabaki we will work timing drills against front and back fudo ken. The second session will dive even deeper into timing sequences in grappling situations. Using Fudo ryu kata from several levels we will combine striking and throws.
