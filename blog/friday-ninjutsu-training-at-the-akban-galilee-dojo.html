Friday Ninjutsu training at the AKBAN Galilee dojo

This Friday we will cover basic Kihon and Kata, Instructor is Dan and working with him is Nimrod. The Techniques:
  • Seigan no Kamae
  • The Tsuki step compared with the cross punch sliding step
  • Jodan yuke, upper level block
  • Analysis and uses of Sakanagare kata
  • Better uchi komi of Koshi waza
  • Yokuto kata as example of striking and grappling sequence
  • Jumonji moto gata as a basis of distance control
  • Koku no kata with different attack directions
