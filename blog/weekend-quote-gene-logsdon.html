Weekend quote - Gene Logsdon

Curiously, only in sports do we agree to eschew technological advances, making rules, for example, to limit the power potential of baseball bats. We understand that technology will ruin our games, but we do not understand that it can also ruin cultures. Gene Logsdon, At Nature's Pace: Farming and the American Dream
