Planned training for 2.10.2013 - AKBAN Jerusalem

This Wednesday we will will be combining the basic framework of joint locks and throws in Ninjutsu. This important session is, every year, one of the cornerstones of understanding grappling work and the relations between different levels of violence in Ninjutsu. We will cover all the basic wrist and shoulder locks. Please, do not forget punching gloves for Kata training. The sessions start with Ninjutsu kamae followed by the himum.
