Weekly Ninjutsu plan for Tel Aviv, Jerusalem and Ramat Hasharon dojo, 12.1.2014-16.1.2014

This Tuesday in Tel Aviv we will be at the temporary dojo at 193 Ben Yehuda St. At all the other dojos we'll be training as usual. We are starting to integrate the reciprocal sequence combos we have been training with into the kata practice from three Ninjutsu systems: Koto ryu, Gyokko ryu and Fudo Ryu. Please do not forget to bring punching gloves as we always validate our Ninjutsu with randori. For questions, please call me at:052-5108747 The sessions start with Ninjutsu kamae followed by the himum. The session ends with Randori. We upload selected Ninjutsu lessons to the Ninjutsu training category at the AKBAN wiki.
