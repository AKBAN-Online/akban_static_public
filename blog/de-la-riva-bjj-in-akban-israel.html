De la Riva BJJ in Akban Israel

A week long intensive seminar saved some Akban Veterans the flight to Rio De Janeiro. Master De la Riva, our personal authority in all matters concerning ground fighting and Brazilian Jiu Jitsu, came to Israel to teach. Gidon Zagher, an Akban Veteran an De la Riva's representative dealt with all the administration and technical matters and brought his teacher to various Akban Dojo all over Israel. It was great, morning till evening full time tutorials and fights. We thank Gideon, who got his black belt, and all the other Akban veterans who edged upward and got purple and blue belts. Most of all we thank De la Riva – Bom dia, mestre De La Riva, e muito obrigado!
14/04/2010
