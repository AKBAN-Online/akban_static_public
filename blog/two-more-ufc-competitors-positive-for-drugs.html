Two more UFC competitors positive for drugs

Both Sean Sherk and Hermes Franca tested positive for drugs after their bout in the UFC 73. While Royce Gracie chooses not to appeal after failing a previous drug test. Gracie said that he would rather loose 2500 $US in fines and be suspended for a year (which is the standard Ruling in these cases) then pay 10000$ for lawyer fees.
