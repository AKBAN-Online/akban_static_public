Krav Maga, Israeli self defence in India

Following the recent terrorist scare after the Mumbai terrorist attack Indian youth are looking for ways to foster their personal security. The Israeli system of Krav Maga seems like a natural choice since it advertises itself as a martial system that is very unique and efficient. If the hype in India will be as strong as the popularity of Krav Maga in USA and Europe then we will see Vicky (Vikram) Kapoor, the head instructor opening many krav Maga training places.
