6 New Gyokko Ryu kata on the Akban wiki

We have resumed the documentation of Gyokko ryu kata. Today I have uploaded six kata from the Chu ryaku no maki level. These kata are in an easy pace to allow training with an additional slow motion repeat at the end. Some of the kata are Muto dori, that is - empty hand against a weapon.

list of new martial art techniques

# Horaku # Dashin # Shien # U jaku # Ko rai # Seito
