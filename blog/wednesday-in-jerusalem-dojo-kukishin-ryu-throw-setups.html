Wednesday in Jerusalem dojo - Kukishin Ryu throws' setups

In the first session we are repeating various Ninjutsu combos, focusing on sabaki and kick combinations. We will explore the Totoku Hyoshi stance and connect it to sabaki. At the second, veteran, session we will be using the Osoto Geri, Osoto gake and O Guruma throws. We are setting these throws with advanced elbow and shoulder arm locks.
