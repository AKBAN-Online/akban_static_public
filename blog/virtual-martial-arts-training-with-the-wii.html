Virtual martial arts training with the Wii

Maybe. Nintendo, the Japanese creator of the motion sensing remote, revolutionized video games and provided the cheap means to combine motion sensing devices and virtual environments. On our field of expertise, Wii now provides the rudimentary abilities to practice boxing. The day students will give up the Dojo and will practice against Bruce Lee and Sakuraba is not far away - it is here. Can technology replace the community and the teaching environment?
