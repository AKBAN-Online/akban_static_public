Tuesday in Tel Aviv dojo - Gyokko and Fudo Ryu

Still at the temporary dojo at 193 Ben Yehuda St. we will start the 1st session with Kuzushi and sabaki uses in Gyokko Ryu kata. We will focus on the circular sabaki that is a characteristic of Gyokko Ryu. At the second session we will use three Kata from Fudo Ryu Dakentaijutsu that, when used in conjuction with the Gyokko ryu sabaki, are useful in Ninjutsu randori. The Kata that are the focus today are Nichigeki, Gekkan and Fubi.
