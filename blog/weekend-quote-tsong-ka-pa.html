Weekend quote - Tsong-ka-pa

From that seal make the seal of the vajra armour. Having spread out the two forefingers, form them in the manner of a vajra (seal 9). Make the armour [in the sense of imagining vajra armour], touching the head, the tops of both shoulders, heart and neck.
