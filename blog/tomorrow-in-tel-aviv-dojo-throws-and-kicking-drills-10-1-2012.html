Tomorrow in Tel Aviv dojo, Throws and kicking drills - 10.1.2012

At the first training session we will go over two Ninjutsu throws combinations. Then we will learn one more kick response from Naname ushiro tai sabaki. At the second session we will explore the Kukishinden Shirabe moguri level kata (still not documented in wiki) and some of its perspectives in Randori. We will repeat, on both sessions, the basic henka from the Bobi no kamae.
