Ninjutsu training this week, 13-17.7.2014

This week we will train as usual even though we'll probably experience rocket fire again. The Jerusalem dojo will be moving back to Tedi stadium dojo till the end of this small war. We have top-grade bomb shelter on the Tedi center ground floor. We also opened up the bomb shelter in Ramat Hasharon dojo and it will allow us a quick response just in case. To the point. We are continuing this week our recap of the technical requirements for the Ninjutsu grade tests. We will focus on throws and shoulder arm locks. I still don't know if we will be doing Niradin weapon training because of some rental issues. Stay safe, breath deeply and don't flinch. See you in the dojo.
