Weekend quote - Snufkin

“I live all over the place” answered Snufkin, and put the coffee pot on the fire. “Today I happen to be here. Tomorrow I will be somewhere else. I wander about as I please. When I find a place I like, I pitch my tent and play my harmonica.” From Moomins: Snufkin's Book of Thoughts, Tove Jansson
