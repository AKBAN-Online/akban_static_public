This Thursday is the first day of the 2013 August Blitz. The Blitz has been with us for many years. At the height of summer, when temperatures are unbearable and many take a rest, we start the most grueling and tough month long event - The Blitz. No special facilities, no expensive equipment, just will power that makes us come again and again to our dojos for a session that consists of sparring.
