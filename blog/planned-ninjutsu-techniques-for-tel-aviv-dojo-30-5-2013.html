Planned Ninjutsu techniques for Tel Aviv dojo - 30.5.2013

We will use modern AKBAN methods to drill in the basics of Ninjutsu kata work - the interdependency of Uke and tori. The basic requirements of breathing, stance and movement, alignment, breathing and above all - awareness will be combined in a reciprocal combo. At the attached clip the genius trainer, Roger Mayweather, teaches these same principles in western boxing. If you are a novice you might have to watch the clip several times to understand the points and the combos drilled. The sessions will start with kamae followed by the himum.
