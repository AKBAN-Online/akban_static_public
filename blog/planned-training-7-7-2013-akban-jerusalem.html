Planned training 7.7.2013 - AKBAN Jerusalem

We will be at Gan Havradim, at the top meadow, near the supreme court building. The two combos we worked with last week are the basis of this session. We will add daken to the combo's sabaki to adapt the sequences to different opponents. We will learn another combo, based on Koku no kata and ki gata of bobi no kamae. Before the randori we will do uchi komi of kani basami. The sessions start with Ninjutsu kamae followed by the himum. The session ends with Randori.
