Beginning of Combo July, 3.7.2013 - AKBAN Jerusalem Ninjutsu

Today is the first day of "Combo July" in AKBAN Jerusalem. The session starts at 18:00 and ends at 20:00. We are moving to the top grass meadow, at the Wohl Rose Park of Jerusalem, opposite the Knesset. Combo July is the intense month that precedes the August combat blitz, a month with no technical lessons, only sparring, full time.
View AKBAN summer 2013 in a larger map So this Wednesday, we will be using the combinations we drilled with this year, and upping the role of uke in accordance with the Ninjutsu kata principles. This new technical frontier will leave a residue of the essence of the main kata levels in our randori. The sessions start with Ninjutsu kamae followed by the himum. The session ends with Randori.
