Planned session for AKBAN Tel Aviv - 23.7.2013

This Tuesday we start from the 5th, distance control, super-combo. We will try to finish at least 3 bifurcations of this combo. The fourth combo, the Kanstesu waza, will be incorporated on both sides with 3 bifurcations each. The three timing combos will get one more bifurcation. Boxing gloves are essential. A reminder: Our dojo has moved up for the summer. We will be training at the third floor of the school's building because of the renovations of our dojo. The sessions will start with kamae followed by the himum. The session ends with Randori. I upload selected Ninjutsu lessons to the Ninjutsu training category at the AKBAN wiki.
