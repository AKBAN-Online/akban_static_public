4.11.2013, AKBAN Ramat Hasharon, Planned Ninjutsu training

Using Tsukitaoshi we will create, with the help of Uke, opportunities for kata from the Kukishinden ryu and especially the Shirabe Moguri level. The session will start with Ninjutsu kamae followed by the himum. We finish the session with full contact Randori. A video summery of this session will be uploaded to Ninjutsu training in video category in the AKBAN wiki.
