Planned Ninjutsu training, AKBAN Ramat Hasharon - 16.9.2013

This Monday session will be mainly rapid retreat and advance using henka, modifications, of Koku and Tatsunami. We will see how the fourth sequence builds a solid engagement layer that prepares for this instances of 'closing the gap - engaging the opponent'. Most of the techniques this monday will be medium level of violence. The session will start with Ninjutsu kamae followed by the himum. We finish the session with full contact Randori. A video summery of this session will be uploaded to Ninjutsu training in video category in the AKBAN wiki.
