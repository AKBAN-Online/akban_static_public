Sunday in Jerusalem dojo - Ushiro Kaiten, Ushiro geri

This Sunday, starting at 18:00 at the Jerusalem dojo, we will use the Ushiro Geri and the Ninjutsu Ushiro Kaiten as a primer for spatial orientation against multiple opponents situation. Ukemi and Kaiten waza in Ninjutsu are used in AKBAN not only as means to dissipate the energy of a fall but in the same way Karuwaza, Ninjutsu acrobatics, is used - as techniques to achieve spatial orientation. In our academy's syllabus a Ninjutsu somersault will be used to prepare the body and vestibular system for the high speed falls and balistic conditions of combat.
