"AKBAN 120"? What is it? Learn about this insanity now

At the hottest and most humid month, after one month Blitz, we engage in a training session that demands not only extreme fitness but also correct and careful technique. Twelve months of the yearly training cycle always finish with the infamous AKBAN 120. In the AKBAN 120 we spar in one minute rounds, different partners each round, for two hours, 120 minutes, non-stop. The "120" will take place in Tel Aviv, this Wednesday, August the 30th, between 6PM-8PM. I'll send the exact location on our WhatsApp groups. As Ran shows in the video, this is AKBAN Tel Aviv in August, be prepared for high humidity. Bring water and protective equipment. See you there,
