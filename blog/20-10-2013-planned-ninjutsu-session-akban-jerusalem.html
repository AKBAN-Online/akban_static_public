20.10.2013 Planned Ninjutsu session - AKBAN Jerusalem

This Sunday we will repeat Tangeki no Kata and other Ki-gata from the beginning stances. Another part of the session will be Nage waza, and especially randori variations of Ganseki Nage and Otoshi. The session will conclude with some intense sparring combos, so we have to remember to put those punching gloves in the dojo bag...
