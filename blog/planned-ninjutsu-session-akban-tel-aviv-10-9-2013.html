Planned Ninjutsu session, AKBAN Tel Aviv - 10.9.2013

A reminder, we are training at our temporary dojo, 193 Ben Yehuda st. 1st floor. This Tuesday we will continue to hone our Uke skills. Using hand armlocks as a precursor, we will relearn correct Ninjutsu rolls, breakfalls and Nagare in grappling situations. We will add to these two sparring oriented kicks. The sessions will start with kamae followed by the himum. The session ends with Randori. I upload selected Ninjutsu lessons to the Ninjutsu training category at the AKBAN wiki.
