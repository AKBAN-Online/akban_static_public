Tuesday in Tel Aviv dojo - 20.11.2012

I am closing the parking lot entrance, so the only entrance will be from the cafeteria door. The Tel Aviv dojo is equipped with a bomb shelter. We start today, as usual, with the Ninjutsu Kamae we repeat every session and after that the Himum. Today we will train on Gyokko ryu techniques and especially Koshijutsu Kamae. The video from this lesson will be uploaded to the wartime Ninjutsu database.
