Planned Ninjutsu session, 17.7.2013 - AKBAN Jerusalem

All the time invested in learning the 3 combos and their main bifurcations will now be put to use with an Uke driven free practice. We will dismantle out of the fourth combo two specific drills, and, if time suffice, will learn the 4th combo, 1st bifurcation. All this time and daken drilling will be balanced by doing uchikomi of several Ninjutsu throws. The sessions start with Ninjutsu kamae followed by the himum. The session ends with Randori.
