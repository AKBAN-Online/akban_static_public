Ninjutsu sessions this week, 28.12.2014-2.1.2015

This Friday, 2.1.2015, we will train in the ECR group. You can ask your instructor, the ECR group (Edge Combat Research) is open to all AKBAN dojos. Last week was dedicated to researching the two basic throws of Kukishinden Ryu. The Friday ECR group even advanced to using these throws in Randori. This week, in all the dojos, we will go further and learn resistance and Henka to these Nage waza concepts. The Advanced Combat Kata groups will utilize these waza in another Shirabe Moguri kata - Yama Otoshi.
