Second and third levels of Kukishin Ryu now on the AKBAN wiki

I had a little more time to write this week, so now the second and third levels, Chuden-gata and Sabaki-gata, of Kukishin Ryu martial art is in the AKBAN wiki, ready and organized. Clicking on the category links above allows a quick scan of all the video techniques on one page, or you can use the lists to explore each technique individually.
