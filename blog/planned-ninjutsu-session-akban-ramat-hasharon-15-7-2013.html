Planned Ninjutsu session, AKBAN Ramat Hasharon - 15.7.2013

This Monday, at Ramat Hasharon dojo, we will try to cram three weeks of gradual super-combo learning into one, hour and a half, session. We will learn combos One to Four and do bifurcations 1.1, 1.2, 1.3, 1.4, 2.1, 2.2, 2.3, 2.4, 3.1, 3.2 and 4.1 The session will start with Ninjutsu kamae followed by the himum. We finish the session with full contact Randori. A video summery of this session will be uploaded to Ninjutsu training in video category in the AKBAN wiki.
