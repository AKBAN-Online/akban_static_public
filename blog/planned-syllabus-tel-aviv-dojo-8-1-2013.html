Planned syllabus, Tel Aviv dojo - 8.1.2013

At the first session we will devote one part for kicks in Randori and conclude with advanced clinch sutemi waza. The second, veteran's session will be Takagi Yoshin Ryu. The sessions will start with Ninjutsu kamae followed by the himum. The sessions conclude with sparring, Randori.
