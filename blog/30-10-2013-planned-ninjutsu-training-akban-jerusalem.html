30.10.2013, Planned Ninjutsu training - AKBAN Jerusalem

At the first session this Wednesday we will go over the extensive kick's syllabus we are using in street situations. The large variety of Geri and Daken allows us to instinctively choose a technique according to the confrontation's level of violence and expands our repertoire beyond highly injurious techniques. At the second, veteran's session we will work our way through Gyokko ryu's kata we are well versed in and see some implementations of Nagare at the higher levels. The sessions start with Ninjutsu kamae followed by the himum. The session ends with Randori. I upload selected Ninjutsu lessons to the Ninjutsu training category at the AKBAN wiki.
