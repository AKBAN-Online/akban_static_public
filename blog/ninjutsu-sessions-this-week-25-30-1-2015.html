Ninjutsu sessions this week, 25-30.1.2015

This Friday, 30.1.2015, 08:00 sharp, we will have ECR group. At the dojos we will continue the multiple opponents scenarios from short ranges. In addition to this we will repeat using kicks while closing distance in randori. The Combat Kata groups will continue working on Kukishinden Ryu Shirabe Moguri Kata. The Tel Aviv CK group will explore Kata preliminaries and exit points using advanced rhythm with Yoni Ben-Dor. We will also check combat language.
