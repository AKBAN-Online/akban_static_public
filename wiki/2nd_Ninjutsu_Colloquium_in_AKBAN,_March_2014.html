2nd Ninjutsu Colloquium in AKBAN, March 2014

By Yossi Sheriff

The March 2014 2nd colloquium is a Koryu meeting that took place in the AKBAN tel Aviv dojo and explored two Gyokko Ryu kata: Danshi and Sakanagare. Instructors presented their perspective for these two Ninjutsu kata and the possibilities of adapting the kata for Randori. The AKBAN colloquiums take place every two months and are open to veterans from all systems.
