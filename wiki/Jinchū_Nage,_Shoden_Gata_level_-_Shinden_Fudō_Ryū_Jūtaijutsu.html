Jinchū Nage, Shoden Gata level - Shinden Fudō Ryū Jūtaijutsu

By Yossi Sheriff

Video of Jinchū Nage



Jinchū Nage, 人中投 , people throw, ג'ינצ'ו נגה, is a kata from the fighting system of Fudo Ryu. It originates at the part of Fudo Ryu that deals with grappling, the Fudō Ryū Jūtaijutsu and is at the basic level, the Shoden Gata.

This kata has been integrated into the Ninjutsu syllabus of the Takamatsu den.
