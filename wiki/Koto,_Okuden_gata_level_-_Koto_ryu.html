Koto, (虎倒), (Kotō), (קוטו נו קאטה, אוקודן גטה - קוטו ריו - נינג'יטסו), (Tiger throw), is a Kata that is a part of the Koto Ryu koryu of Ninjutsu, It belongs to the fourth Okuden level.

Description of Koto no kata, okuden gata level

Uke cuts with a tento to the left side of tori's neck. Tori does sabaki, blocks with jodan yuke does a happa and throws uke by twisting his neck.
