Hicho, (飛鳥), (היצ'ו נו קאטה - קוטו ריו - נינג'יטסו), (Flying bird), is a Kata that is a part of the Koto Ryu koryu of Ninjutsu, It belongs to the third Chuden level.

Description of Hicho no kata - Koto ryu

While walking against a standing or walking opponent, one stikes the face with Kuma te and then continues to topple the opponent with a Ninjutsu Push kick.
