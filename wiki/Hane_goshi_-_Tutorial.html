Hane goshi - Tutorial

By Yossi Sheriff

Video tutorial of Hane goshi


Hane goshi is a Judo technique that utilises the hip movement to throw the opponent.

How to do a hane goshi

Pull the opponent on the hip by pulling hard with both hands toward your side. At the time when the opponent is on the hip the same side leg is extended backwards while the hands make a pulling movement forward and down.
