Video of Ranfu no kata - Kukishinden ryu


Ranfu (乱風), (Chaos wind), (רנפו), also written as Rapuu or Ranfū is a Kata.

This form is part of the Kukishin ryu Dakentaijutsu (九鬼神流打拳体術) koryu, belonging to the Sabaki gata level.

Ranfu is a part of the syllabus of the Takamatsu den schools of Ninjutsu: Bujinkan, Jinenkan, AKBAN.
