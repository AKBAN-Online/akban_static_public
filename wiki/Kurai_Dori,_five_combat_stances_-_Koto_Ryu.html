Kurai Dori, five combat stances - Koto Ryu

By Yossi Sheriff

Kurai Dori, Positioning, (位取), (קוראי דורי), is the name of five Ninjutsu atitudes in Koto Ryu martial system in the Koppojutsu Specialization (虎倒流骨法術).

List of five combat atitudes of Koto ryu

  1. Migi Seigan no kamae - One side, right leg forward
  2. Hidari Seigan no kamae - One side, left leg forward
  3. Hoko no kamae, Bear stance - Ninjutsu
  4. Hira ichimonji no kamae, horizontal stance - Ninjutsu
  5. Bobi no kamae
