Kihon happo

By Yossi Sheriff

Kihon Happo (基本八法型), is the name for basic essential techniques in Bujinkan school for Ninjutsu. The techniques for the Kihon Happo are

  1. Ichimonji no kamae (Sui no kata) from Category:Techniques - Gyokko Ryu: This is also called Sui no kata - Against a tsuki a high block, you do jodan yuke and a Shuto ken to the neck.
  2. Jumonji no kamae from Category:Techniques - Gyokko Ryu: Against a Tsuki block Jodan yuke and a Boshi ken to the Butsumetsu Kyusho.
  3. Hicho no kamae from Category:Techniques - Gyokko Ryu: Against right punch to the stomach, do a Hicho no kata, left foot raised, Gedan yuke. Left Kick to Uke's armpit or ribs and Shuto ken to collar bone.
  4. Omote gyaku from Category:Techniques - Takagi Yoshin Ryu - An outer wrist joint lock.
  5. Ura gyaku from Category:Techniques - Takagi Yoshin Ryu - An inner wrist joint lock.
  6. Hon gyaku from Category:Techniques - Takagi Yoshin Ryu - A twisting wrist joint lock.
  7. Gosha dori from Category:Techniques - Takagi Yoshin Ryu - An elbow, shoulder joint lock
  8. Ganseki nage from [:Category:Techniques - Fudo Ryu]] - An elbow lock and a throw
