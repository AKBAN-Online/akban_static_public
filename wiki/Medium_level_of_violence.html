Medium level of violence

By Yossi Sheriff

Medium level of violence is an attribute of a meta perspective on the AKBAN wiki that denotes a technique that initially causes intermediate harm and might be less controllable. Level of violence is a meta perspective term used in AKBAN to tag techniques by their results rather then by their constituents.
