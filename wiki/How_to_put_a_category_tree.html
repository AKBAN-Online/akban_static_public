How to put a category tree

By Yossi Sheriff

Here are several examples of usage of the category tree on the Akban wiki.


Writing <categorytree> Budo Ninjutsu techniques </categorytree> gives the the user, after saving the page the root category.

The user can press the "+" to expand any one on the list, try, for example, the "Blocks" since it has a sub category... :

► Budo Ninjutsu techniques
no subcategories


Writing <categorytree hideroot=on> Budo Ninjutsu techniques </categorytree> gives the subcategories without the root:



Writing <categorytree mode=pages> Budo Ninjutsu techniques </categorytree> gives all pages, try pressing the "+":
