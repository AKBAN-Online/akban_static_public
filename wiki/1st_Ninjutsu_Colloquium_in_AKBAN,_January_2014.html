1st Ninjutsu Colloquium in AKBAN, January 2014

By Yossi Sheriff

The January 2014 colloquium is a martial research oriented meeting that took place in the AKBAN tel Aviv dojo and revolved around several kata from koto ryu. Instructors presented a mixed perspective on the degree of adaptability of traditional koryu kata to modern dojo scenarios.

The colloquiums take place every month and are open to veterans from all systems.
