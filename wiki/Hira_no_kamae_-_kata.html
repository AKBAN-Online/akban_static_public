Hira no kamae - kata

By Yossi Sheriff

As sword comes down change into ichimonji no kamae and roll away.

Note: Roll away straight at ninety degrees. This is a fundamental. Carefully seeing the interval between you and the weapon. This sort of training is a most important element in taihenjutsu. {{
