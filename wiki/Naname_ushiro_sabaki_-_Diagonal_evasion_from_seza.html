Naname ushiro sabaki - Diagonal evasion from seza

By Yossi Sheriff

Video of Diagonal evasion from seza


Naname ushiro sabaki - Diagonal evasion from seza, (התחמקות מישיבת סזה באלכסון לאחור), is a sitting technique. This technique is used in some ninjutsu Kata.
