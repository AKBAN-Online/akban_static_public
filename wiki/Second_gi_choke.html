Second gi choke

By Yossi Sheriff

Second gi choke or Kata Hajime, is a part of the syllabus of Brazilian Jiu Jitsu, Judo and Budo Ninjutsu. This choke hold is very powerful and very useful as it is applied from the opponent's rear - and he can do little to resist it once the hold is complete. In addition to the fact that the user of the technique is very well protected as he is applying the choke from the opponent's back- the hold is very deadly as it is both a blood and air choke hold.
