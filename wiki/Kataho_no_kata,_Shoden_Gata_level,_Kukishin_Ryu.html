Description of kataho

Both in Seigan. Uke attacks with right Tsuki, left Tsuki, right push kick and left push kick. Tori blocks the Tsuki with two Jodan yuke and strikes the right kick with a right punch in the shin bone Kyuso and the left kick with a right hand Gedan yuke. Uke attacks with right Tsuki, tori blocks Jodan Yuke and proceeds to do a modified Oni Kudaki armlock on tori's right hand.
