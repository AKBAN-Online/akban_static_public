Detant Niradin lessons with Yossi Sheriff

By Yossi Sheriff

Detant Niradin lessons with Yossi Sheriff (שיעורי דטאנט ניראדין עם יוסי שריף) lists the daily lessons of the Detant Niradin system, as thought by Yossi Sheriff in the time of the Corona crisis.

Detant-Niradin is the integration of two systems for harmonious development: Detant and Niradin. Niradin is a deep moving away system. Although it shares similarities with many systems, Niradin's core rational is distinct.

The systems that influenced Niradin system are Chinese Chi Gong, Lo Ka Pa Fat and various soft and hard Wu Shu systems learned by Nir Adin Unger, the founder, during his years of residence in Macau and China. Pranayama, Bhakti, Raja and Hatha Yoga were integrated into Niradin during Nir's years in India.

Gaby Frishlander and Yossi Sheriff learned under Nir for years and in a synergic cooperative effort, together with Nir continued to develop the system and translate it for students.

Detant syllabus in AKBAN was developed by Yossi Sheriff, a certified Niradin and Ninjutsu teacher, and teaches system 2 syllabus of regulating emotion and mindfulness through body.
