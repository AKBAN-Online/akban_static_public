Do gaeshi no kata, Shoden Omote Gata level, Takagi Yoshin Ryū Jūtaijutsu

By Yossi Sheriff

Video of Do Gaeshi no kata

Do gaeshi, 洞返, hollow capture, (דו גאשי) is a sitting kata from Takagi Yoshin Ryu Ninjutsu done from Seza position.

Description of do gaeshi no kata

Both Opponents sit in Seiza

  • Uke: Attacks with right Tsuki (punching)
  • Tori: Gets up on left foot and with his right hand grabs uke's right hand, hitting with a left Ura shuto to uke's jaw, and grabs the inside of uke's gi. Tori then pulls uke and trips him on his back using tori's knee. Then Tori pulls uke and turns him on his stomach using tori's left leg as leverage on uke's right elbow, finishing with the left knee on the upper arm of uke, holding him on the floor, and choking him by pulling the gi upwards.
