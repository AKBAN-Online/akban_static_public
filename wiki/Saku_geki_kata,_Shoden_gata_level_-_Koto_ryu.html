Sakugeki, (Saku geki), (搾撃), (סקו גקי נו קאטה - קוטו ריו - נינג'יטסו), (Squeeze attack) is a kata of the Koto Ryu koryu in the Takamatsu Den schools of Ninjutsu, It belongs to the basic Shoden level.

Description of Saku geki no kata

Uke tries to grab tori with his right hand, tori then grabs uke's right hand with his left hand and attacks with boshi ken to the neck and a sweep to uke's right keg with his left leg.
