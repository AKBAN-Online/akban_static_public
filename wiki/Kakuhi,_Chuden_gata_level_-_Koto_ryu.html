Kakuhi, (飛倒), (קקוהי נו קאטה - קוטו ריו - נינג'יטסו), (holding the jump), is a Kata that is a part of the Koto Ryu koryu of Ninjutsu, It belongs to the third Chuden level.

Description of Kakuhi no kata

Uke hols tori's lapels with both hands, tori performs Omote gyaku Joint lock on uke's right hand while punching uke in the chin with Fudo ken and then kick uke in the stomach.
