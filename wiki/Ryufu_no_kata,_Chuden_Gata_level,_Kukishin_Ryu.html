Ryufu (龍風), (Dragon wind) otherwise named Yanagi Kaze (柳風), (Willow wind), (ריו פו), is a Kata.

This form is part of the Kukishin ryu Dakentaijutsu (九鬼神流打拳体術) koryu, belonging to the Chuden gata level.

Ryufu is a part of the syllabus of the Takamatsu den schools of Ninjutsu: Bujinkan, Jinenkan, AKBAN.
