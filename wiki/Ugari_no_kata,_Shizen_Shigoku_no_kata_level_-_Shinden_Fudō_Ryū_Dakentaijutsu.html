Ugari, 鶉刈, quail cut, אוגארי, is a kata that forms part of the Shizen Shigoku level of Fudo Ryu Daken Tai Jutsu learned as a part of the Ninjutsu syllabus in Jinenkan, Bujinkan and AKBAN.


Ugari Kata description

Uke grabs tori's lapel and sleeve and throws tori with Osoto gari. Tori grabs uke's left armpit while falling and then throws uke over his head with Yoko sutemi. Tori continues to roll sideways and mounts uke, finishing with a Katata Jime choke with his left forearm on uke's neck.
