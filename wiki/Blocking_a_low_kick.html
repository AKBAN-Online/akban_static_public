The Block (בלוק) is the name of counter kick technique that is used as a Muay Tai technique that is classified as an leg blocking technique. This technique is also used in Ninjutsu but is called Keri Kaishi.

Description of Blocking a low kick

Using the shin, lift the leg straight up and block the opponnent's low kick. Keeping the hands high helps protect the face. The opposite hand is thrust in front of the opponent's face to hide his field of vision.
