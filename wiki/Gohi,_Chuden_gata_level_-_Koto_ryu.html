Gohi, Nezumi hi, (גוהי נו קאטה - קוטו ריו - נינג'יטסו), (Flying rat), is a Kata that is a part of the Koto Ryu koryu of Ninjutsu, It belongs to the third Chuden level.

Description of Gohi no kata

Against a walking Uke, in walking stride, one hits the opponent's pressure point with a Boshi ken, Shikan ken or a Goshin tsuki.
