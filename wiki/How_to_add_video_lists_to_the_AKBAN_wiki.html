How to add video lists to the AKBAN wiki

By Yossi Sheriff

On the AKBAN wiki we have two kinds of video lists that are generated on the page

  1. Numbered list - Used to manually generate video thumbnails from an edited, hand picked, list
  2. Category list - Used to automatically generate video thumbnails from the contents of the articles in a category.

Numbered, manual video list on the AKBAN wiki

Create such a list by writing


<videolistfromlist> *[[Front kick, cross punch, front hook and a knee]] *[[Jusan no kata no kumi jo, Jo, Aikido]] </videolistfromlist>


This will create a video list that contains these two articles. Every page in in the article list that contains a video generates a thumbnail

Front kick, cross punch, front hook and a knee

Jusan no kata no kumi jo, Jo, Aikido



Category video list

This list creates a thumbnail video list from the articles inside a specified category that contain videos. Create such a list by writing


<videolistnew> Tewaza-gata, kukishin ryu Dakentaijutsu </videolistnew>


This will create automatic video list from '''category:Tewaza-gata, kukishin ryu Dakentaijutsu'''. Every page in in the article list that contains a video generates a thumbnail

Omote Gyaku Ken Sabaki, twisting wrist lock from a punch - Ninjutsu

Take ori

Ura oni kudaki

Muso dori, elbow break, Ninjutsu
