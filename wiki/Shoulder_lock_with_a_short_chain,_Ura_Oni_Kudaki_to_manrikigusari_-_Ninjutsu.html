Shoulder lock with a short chain, Ura Oni Kudaki to manrikigusari - Ninjutsu

By Yossi Sheriff

Ura Oni Kudaki to manrikigusari, short chain shoulder lock, (אורה אוני קודקי שרשרת), is a shoulder joint lock from the Ninjutsu weapon syllabus of AKBAN. It is a derivative of Oni Kudaki.
