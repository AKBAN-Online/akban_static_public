Summary of Basic clinch

This is a Muay Tai technique that is classified as an close range technique.

Description of Basic clinch technique

Using one hand with its fingers joined, cup the back of the opponent's neck. With the other hand block the bicep region of the other hand. The knees must be slightly bent and flexible and the back straight.

keep your elobows tucked in so you can shove as well as pull your opponent.

The clinch can be used to control the opponent's movements and balance and to prevent him from punching with his arms.
