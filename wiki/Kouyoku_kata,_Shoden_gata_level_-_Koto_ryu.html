Kouyoku, Koyoku, (抗抒), (קויוקו נו קאטה), (counter and eliminate), is a Kata that is a part of the Koto Ryu koryu in Ninjutsu. It belongs to the Shoden level.

Description of Kouyoku no kata

uke and tori in ichi monji no kamae, uke attacks with tsuki, tori blocks with jodan yuke, bushi ken to uke's stomach then performs gan seki nage on the right hand.
