Fudoza no Kamae - Ninjutsu


Video of Nanamae ushiro sabaki from fudoza


Fudoza, 不動坐, immovable seat (פודוזה), or Fudosa. This posture is part of Ninjutsu syllabus, and is used in several Fudo Ryu and Takagi Yoshin Ryu.
