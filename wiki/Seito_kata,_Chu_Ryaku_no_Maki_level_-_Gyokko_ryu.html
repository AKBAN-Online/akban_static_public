Video of Seito no kata


Seito, (鯖倒), (Mackerel fall), (סייטו) is a kata from the middle level of Gyokku ryu. This kata belongs to syllabus of the Takamatzu den Ninjutsu kata.

Description of Seito no Kata

Uke attacks with right tsuki, tori does inside jodan yuke. Uke does a right push kick, tori hits the leg with right fudo ken. Uke does a left tsuki, tori grabs the wrist with left hand and attacks the face with a right kuma te, grabs the left shoulder, delivers a right kick to the ribs and throws uke in a variant of oni kudaki.
