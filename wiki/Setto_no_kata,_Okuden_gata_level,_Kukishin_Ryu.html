Video of Setto kata (Yuki Taoshi) - Kukishinden ryu


Setto (瀬戸), (Strait), (סטו קאטה), also named Yuki Taoshi, (雪倒), is a Kata.

This combat - form is a part of the Kukishin ryu Dakentaijutsu (九鬼神流打拳体術) koryu, belonging to the lower advanced level Okuden gata.

Setto is a part of the syllabus of the Takamatsu den schools of Ninjutsu: Bujinkan, Jinenkan, AKBAN.
