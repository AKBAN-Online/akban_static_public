Knife stances - Ninjutsu

By Yossi Sheriff

Video of seven basic Kunai and Tento stances


Knife stances (עמידות סכין) in Ninjutsu are devided to 7 basic ones and 5 intermediate. The knives used are the Ninjutsu Kunai and Tento. The stances allow the practitioner to adapt his physical and emotional position in a Fight.
