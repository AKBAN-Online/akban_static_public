Juji gatame - Arm bar - from knee on stomach

By Yossi Sheriff

Juji gatame from knee on stomach is an armbar intended to break the elbow on the ground. Juji gatame is a part of the syllabus of Brazilian Jiu Jitsu, Judo and Budo Ninjutsu.
