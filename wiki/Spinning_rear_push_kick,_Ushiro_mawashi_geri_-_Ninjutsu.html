Spinning rear push kick, Ushiro mawashi geri - Ninjutsu

By Yossi Sheriff

Ushiro Mawashi, spinning back kick (אושירו מאוושי) is a Ninjutsu kick that uses the momentum of the rotating body to increase the speed of a back kick and surprise the opponent. This kick is prevalent in many Martial sports.
