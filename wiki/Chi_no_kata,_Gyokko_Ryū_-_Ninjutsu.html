Chi no kata (地の型), (צ'י נו קאטה - גיוקו ריו - נינג'יטסו) is one of the five kata comprising the important Go Gyo of the Ninjutsu strategy. For the Chinese philosophical term related to martial arts and healing see - Ch'i.


Description of chi no kata

From shizen tai, gedan yuke inside, tsuki step to strike gorin or sugetsu with goshin tsuki.
