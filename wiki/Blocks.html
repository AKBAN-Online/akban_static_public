Blocks

By Yossi Sheriff

Blocking is the act of physically deflecting the opponent's strike. Ninjutsu blocks can roughly be divided into hand blocks and leg blocks - Keri Kaishi.

In Ninjutsu, evading an opponent's strike is primarily based on body movement Tai Sabaki.
