10 January 2013, The reverse Gi grip and its uses in Randori - part 1 - Ninjutsu training lessons

By Yossi Sheriff

Use this page to be up to date with current Ninjutsu sessions in the dojo - Keep training!.

These are January the 10th Ninjutsu training videos and Ninjutsu techniques from the Tel Aviv dojo learned at January, 2013.

Several throws were explored from the reverse grip so common in Kukishinden ryu: Ninjutsu underhook, Osoto gake, Seoe and advanced ura of Kata Guruma. All these throws are common in Randori.
