Kinteki sokushi geri - Kick to the groin

By Yossi Sheriff

Kinteki sokushi geri or Kin geri (בעיטה לביצים) is a Ninjutsu front crotch kick. This kick is aimed to the groin and uses either the back of the foot, the ball of the foot or the Sokushi, tips of toes, to hit the opponent's groin.
