Ura ashi geri - Sole of foot kick to the groin

By Yossi Sheriff

Ura ashi geri also known as Sukui Keri, 掬蹴, (בעיטת סוליה לביצים) is a Ninjutsu special crotch kick and a part of Happō Keri – Eight-directional kicking drill.

This kick is aimed to the groin and uses the sole of foot, to hit the opponent's privates from close range. This kick is suitable to close range throws work.
