Hitohiro Saito

By Yossi Sheriff

Hitohiro Saito is an aikido teacher and the headmaster of Iwama Shin-Shin Aiki Shuren-kai.

Hitohiro started to learn Aikido at a yonug age, as a direct student of Morihei Ueshiba, the founder of aikido

After Ueshiba's death in 1969, Hitohiro became head teacher of the Iwama dojo in 1986 and remained so until 2004 when he separated from the Aikikai organization and formed his own group.
