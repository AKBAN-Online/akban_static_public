5th Ninjutsu Colloquium in AKBAN, October 2014

By Yossi Sheriff

The 5th AKBAN Ninjutsu Colloquium took place in AKBAN Honbu dojo at October the 24th, and focused on one advanced Kata from the martial system of Kukisinden. The kata was Gyaku Nage. For a comprehensive look on all the Ninjutsu Colloquia, go to the category.

This open seminar researched issues and perspectives of awareness, attack syncopes and technical challenges at grappling distances. The issues will be explore from the perspective of fighting and the perspective of martial art.
