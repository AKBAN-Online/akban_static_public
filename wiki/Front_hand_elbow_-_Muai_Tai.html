Front hand elbow - Muai Tai

By Yossi Sheriff

Summary of Elbow cross

This is a Muay Tai technique that is classified as an elbow technique.

Description of Front hand elbow technique

While maintaining a wide stance, take a big step forward and hit the opponent's face from the side using the front elbow. The palm of the punching arm stays near the face to protect from hooks while entering.
