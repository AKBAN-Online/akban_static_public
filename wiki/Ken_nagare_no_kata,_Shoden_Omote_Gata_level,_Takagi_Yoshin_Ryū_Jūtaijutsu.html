Video of Ken Nagare (Ken Nagashi)

Ken nagare, 拳流, Fist flow, (קן נגארה), sometimes called Ken Nagashi, is a basic kata from the Takagi Yoshin Ryu.

Description of Ken Nagare

  • Uke attacks with a tsuki from seigan no kamae.
  • Tori waits in shizen tai no kamae, does a sabaki diagonally forward to the side and catches uke's hand from outside.
  • Tori does a muso dori arm lock on uke's elbow
  • Tori falls to zenpo ukemi.
