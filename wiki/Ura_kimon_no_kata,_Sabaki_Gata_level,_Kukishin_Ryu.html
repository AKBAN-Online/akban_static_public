Video of Ura kimon no kata


Ura kimon (裏鬼門), (Reverse demon's gate), (אורה קימון), is a Kata.

This form is part of the Kukishin ryu Dakentaijutsu (九鬼神流打拳体術) koryu, belonging to the Sabaki gata level.

Ura kimon is a part of the syllabus of the Takamatsu den schools of Ninjutsu: Bujinkan, Jinenkan, AKBAN.
