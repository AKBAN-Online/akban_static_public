Fudo, 不動, immovable, פודו, is a kata that forms part of the Shizen Shigoku level of Fudo Ryu Daken Tai Jutsu learned as a part of the Ninjutsu syllabus in Jinenkan, Bujinkan and AKBAN.


Description of Fudo no kata

The Fudo gata reaches Take ori hand lock from various starting points.
