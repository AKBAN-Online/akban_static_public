Category:TSKSR, Omote no iai

By Yossi Sheriff

Omote no iai category, (אומוטה נו איאיי), is a Kata category in the Akban-wiki. This level is covers all the forms and techniques of drawing a sword, while sitting in position in Tenshin Shōden Katori Shintō-ryū (天真正伝香取神道流).
