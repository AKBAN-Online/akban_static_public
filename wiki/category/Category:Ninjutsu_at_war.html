Category:Ninjutsu at war

By Yossi Sheriff

Use this page to be up to date with current Ninjutsu sessions in the dojo - Keep training!.

The Ninjutsu at war category, (השלמת אימונים בזמן מלחמה), lists all the Ninjutsu techniques' tutorials that we started to document during security situations that prevented our AKBAN veterans from coming to the training session.

Even though the security situation and the rockets that have been fired on the AKBAN Tel Aviv dojo has turned into a cease fire, we will continue to upload lessons to this category until there will be peace in this part of the glob.
