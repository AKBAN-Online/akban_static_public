Gogyo no Tachi category, (גוגיו נו טאצ'י), is a Kata category in the Akban-wiki. This level is basic level that covers all the forms and techniques of the Omote gata in Tenshin Shōden Katori Shintō-ryū (天真正伝香取神道流).
