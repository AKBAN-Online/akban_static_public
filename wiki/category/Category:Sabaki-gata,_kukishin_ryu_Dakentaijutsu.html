Category:Sabaki-gata, kukishin ryu Dakentaijutsu

By Yossi Sheriff

Sabaki Gata category (捌型), (סבאקי גטה), is a Kata category in the Akban-wiki. This level is the advanced intermediate level that covers all the forms and techniques of the sabaki gata in Kukishin ryu Dakentaijutsu (九鬼神流打拳体術).
