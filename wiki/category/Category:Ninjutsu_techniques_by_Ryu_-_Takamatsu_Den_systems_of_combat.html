Category:Ninjutsu techniques by Ryu - Takamatsu Den systems of combat

By Yossi Sheriff

The Takamatsu den category lists all the techniques of the ancient Japanese Koryu systems integrated into Ninjutsu.

The Ninjutsu by Ryu category lists the Ninjutsu kata and techniques thought by Takamatsu to Masaaki Hatsumi, the father of current Ninjutsu.


These techniques come from the six Ryu practiced in Takamazu den - the schools stemming from Takamatsu.
