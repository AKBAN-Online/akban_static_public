Category:Ken suburi techniques, Aikido

By Yossi Sheriff

The Ken suburi category lists all the videos, techniques and moves of the Ken suburi weapon training in the martial art of Aikido. The Ken is specially balanced wooden replica of the samurai sword, the Katana.
