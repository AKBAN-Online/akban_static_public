Category:Shizen Shigoku no kata level, Fudo Ryu Dakentaijutsu

By Yossi Sheriff

Shizen Shigoku no kata category, 自然至極之型, natural hell forms, is a Fudo Ryu level of Ninjutsu techniques that is encompassed in the Shinden Fudō Ryū Dakentaijutsu.

In this category (רובד שיזן שיגוקו קאטה, פודו ריו) reside all the techniques and kata of the Shizen Sigoku no kata of Fudo Ryu.
