Category:Katame waza - grappling techniques

By Yossi Sheriff

There are three categories of katame waza -- grappling techniques.

  • osae waza -- pinning techniques. Control opponent and restrict his movement.
  • shime waza -- choking techniques. Application of pressure to the windpipe or arteries of the neck.
  • kansetsu waza -- joint lock techniques. Application of pressure to the joint.
