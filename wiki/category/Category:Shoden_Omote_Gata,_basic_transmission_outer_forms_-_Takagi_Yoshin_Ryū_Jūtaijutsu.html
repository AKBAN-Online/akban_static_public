Category:Shoden Omote Gata, basic transmission outer forms - Takagi Yoshin Ryū Jūtaijutsu

By Yossi Sheriff

Shoden Omote Gata category, 初伝表型, is the basic kata level in Takagi Yoshin Ryū Jūtaijutsu. It lists thirteen kata that deal with grappling and striking. The Shoden Omote Gata (שודן אומוטה גטה, טקאגי יושין ריו ג'וטאיג'וטסו) forms an important part of Takagi Yoshin Ryū training syllabus.
