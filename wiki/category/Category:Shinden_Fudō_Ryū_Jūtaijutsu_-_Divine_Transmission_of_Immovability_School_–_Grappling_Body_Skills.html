Category:Shinden Fudō Ryū Jūtaijutsu - Divine Transmission of Immovability School – Grappling Body Skills

By Yossi Sheriff

Shinden Fudō Ryū Jūtaijutsu, 神傳不動流柔体術, שינדן פודו ריו ג'ו טאיג'וטסו, is a part of Shinden Fudo Ryu that specializes in grappling and Joint locks.

Fudo Ryu Jūtaijutsu, is an advanced system that together with Shinden Fudō Ryū Dakentaijutsu is learned in the Takamatsu den and has been integrated into the Takamatsu den's Ninjutsu syllabus.
