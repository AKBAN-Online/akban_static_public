Category:Weapons

By Yossi Sheriff

The weapon category lists all the video, techniques and kata that have been documented on the AKBAN wiki and the general background and description of these weapons.

Though we emphasize traditional weapons we also document modern weapons with practice that have been influenced by Martial arts insights.


For the Traditional Weapon techniques list and videos go to the Traditional weapons portal

The integration of modern weapons' practice is managed in AKBAN using the training model of the Methodical pyramid.
