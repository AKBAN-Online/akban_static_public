Category:Techniques - Takagi Yoshin Ryu Jutaijutsu

By Yossi Sheriff

Takagi Yoshin Ryū Jūtaijutsu category, 高木揚心流柔体術, lists, with video, all the kata of Takagi Yoshin Ryu. The Takagi Yoshin Ryū is a Takamatsu den martial system, the Jūtaijutsu of this koryu emphasizes joint locks, throws and daken.
