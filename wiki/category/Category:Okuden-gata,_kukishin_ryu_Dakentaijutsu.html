Category:Okuden-gata, kukishin ryu Dakentaijutsu

By Yossi Sheriff

Okuden gata category (奥伝型), (אוקודן גטה), is a Kata category in the Akban-wiki.

This level is the upper advanced intermediate level that covers all the forms and techniques of the sabaki gata in Kukishin ryu Dakentaijutsu (九鬼神流打拳体術).
