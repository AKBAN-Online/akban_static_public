Category:Eri Jime Kata, lapel strangle forms - Takagi Yoshin Ryū Jūtaijutsu

By Yossi Sheriff

Eri Jime Kata category, 襟締型, lists the strangles and chokes kata in Takagi Yoshin Ryū Jūtaijutsu Ninjutsu. It includes eight kata.

The Eri Jime Kata (ארי ג'ימה קטה, קאטות חניקה, טקאגי יושין ריו ג'וטאיג'וטסו) forms an intermediate level of Takagi Yoshin Ryū training syllabus.
