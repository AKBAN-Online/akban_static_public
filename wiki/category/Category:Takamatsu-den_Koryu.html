Category:Takamatsu-den Koryu

By Yossi Sheriff

The Takamatsu den category lists the Ryu taught by Takamatsu to Yoshiaki (later Masaaki) Hatsumi. On the basis of these techniques Hatsumi founded the Bujinkan school for martial arts. Later years saw two of his students forming schools of their own. These are Shoto Tanemura who founded Genbukan and Fumio Manaka who founded Jinenkan.

Ninjutsu Techniques by Ryu: Takamatsu den ancient systems

  • Shinden Fudo Ryu techniques, 神傳不動流打 - The immovable god school
  • Takagi Yoshin Ryu, 高木揚心流
