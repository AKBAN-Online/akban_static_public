Category:Ten no Kata level, Fudo Ryu Dakentaijutsu

By Yossi Sheriff

Ten no kata category, 天之型, Heaven forms, is a Fudo Ryu level of Ninjutsu techniques that is encompassed in the Shinden Fudō Ryū Dakentaijutsu.

In this category (רובד טן נו קאטה, פודו ריו) reside all the techniques and kata of the Ten no kata level of Fudo Ryu.
