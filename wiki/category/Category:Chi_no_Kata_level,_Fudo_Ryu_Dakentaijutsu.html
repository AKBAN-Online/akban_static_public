Category:Chi no Kata level, Fudo Ryu Dakentaijutsu

By Yossi Sheriff

Chi no Kata category, 地之型, earth forms, (צ'י נו קאטה) is a Fudo Ryu level of Ninjutsu techniques that is encompassed in the Shinden Fudō Ryū Dakentaijutsu.

The hidden principle behind this level of techniques is Biken Isshun, 秘拳一瞬, concealed quick fist.

In this category (רובד צ'י נו קאטה, פודו ריו) reside all the techniques and kata of the Chi no kata level of Fudo Ryu.
