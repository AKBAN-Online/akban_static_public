Category:Tachi ai batto jutsu techniques

By Yossi Sheriff

Tachi ai batto jutsu category, (טאצ'י איי באטו ג'וטסו), is a Kata category in the Akban-wiki. This level is covers all the forms and techniques of drawing a sword, while standing or walking in Tenshin Shōden Katori Shintō-ryū (天真正伝香取神道流).
