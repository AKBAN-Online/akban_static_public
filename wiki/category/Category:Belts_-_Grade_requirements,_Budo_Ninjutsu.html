Category:Belts - Grade requirements, Budo Ninjutsu

By Yossi Sheriff

The belt exams' syllabus category lists all the Belt exam techniques and requirements in AKBAN. The prerequisite is dojo time, and the average time to acquire the expertise for the Black Belt in the basic Ninjutsu techniques is at least 12 years.

List of all the Grades in the AKBAN Ninjutsu academy

  • Yellow belt, 5th kyu, 五級
  • Orange belt, 4th Kyu, 四級
  • Green belt, 3rd Kyu, 三級
  • Blue belt, 2nd Kyu, 二級
  • Brown belt, 1st Kyu, 一級
  • Black belt, 1st Dan, 初段
  • Black belt, 2nd Dan, 二段
  • Black Belt, 3rd Dan, 三段
  • Black belt, 4th Dan, 四段
  • Black belt 5th Dan, 五段
