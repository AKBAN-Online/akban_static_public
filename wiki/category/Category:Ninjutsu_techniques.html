Category:Ninjutsu techniques

By Yossi Sheriff

The Ninjutsu techniques category (קטגוריה: טכניקות נינג'יטסו), lists all the videos of Ninjutsu techniques that have been uploaded to the Akban wiki. There are currently more than 1000 Ninjutsu techniques and Kata with video and we write and video new ones every week.

For an extensive Ninjutsu techniques list and videos go to the Ninjutsu techniques portal

You must have Javascript enabled in your browser to use the full capabilities of this wiki page. You can browse the categories below either by clicking on the links or by pressing the + sign near the category. Pressing the + sign will open a sub level in the tree of techniques.
