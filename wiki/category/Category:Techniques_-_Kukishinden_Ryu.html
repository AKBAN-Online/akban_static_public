Category:Techniques - Kukishinden Ryu

By Yossi Sheriff

The Kukishin Ryu (九鬼神流), Techniques category (שיטת קוקישין - טכניקות) includes all the martial arts techniques and katas of Kukishinden Ryu, one of the Takamatsu den systems and a mainstay in Akban syllabus.
