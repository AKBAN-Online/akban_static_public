Category:Shoden Ura Gata, basic transmission inner forms - Takagi Yoshin Ryū Jūtaijutsu

By Yossi Sheriff

Shoden Ura Gata category, 初伝裏型, is the basic inner kata level in Takagi Yoshin Ryū Jūtaijutsu Ninjutsu. It includes twenty kata that deal with grappling and striking. The Shoden Ura Gata (שודן אורה גטה, טקאגי יושין ריו ג'וטאיג'וטסו) forms an intermediate basic level of Takagi Yoshin Ryū training syllabus.
