Tenchi (天地), (Heaven and earth), (טן צ'י), is a Kata. This two persons' form is part of the Kukishin ryu Dakentaijutsu (九鬼神流打拳体術) koryu, belonging to the Chuden gata level.

Tenchi is part of the syllabus of the Takamatsu den schools of Ninjutsu: Bujinkan, Jinenkan and AKBAN.
