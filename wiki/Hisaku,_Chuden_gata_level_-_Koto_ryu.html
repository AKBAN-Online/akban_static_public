Hisaku, (Hissaku), (飛搾), (היסקו נו קאטה - קוטו ריו - נינג'יטסו), (Flying clinch), is a Kata that is a part of the Koto Ryu koryu of Ninjutsu, It belongs to the third Chuden level.

Description of Hissaku no kata, koto ryu

tori Attacks with Happa to both ears then jumps on uke with kikaku ken, slides down to the ground, grabs uke's ankols and taking uke down by pulling the ankols forward, then kicking uke's ribs.
