Jō

By Yossi Sheriff

The Jō 杖, (ג'ו), is a strong wooden staff made from Japanese oak. It is used by many Japanese martial arts, Ninjutsu included, but is seen all over the world and is considered a common weapon.

The length of the Jō can be from 110 cm to 180 cm.
