Tai hodoki - Escape from rear bear hug

By Yossi Sheriff

Video of 1st release from a rear hug


Description of the Technique

In the first scenario the opponent applies the bears hug around both torso and arms. To release himself of the hold- the user first hits the opponent's head with the back of his head. Next- while the opponent's head is thrown backwards- the user lowers his stance by placing his feet further apart and bending his knees, raises both of his elbows and pushes his back side into the opponent's stomach- pushing him away.
