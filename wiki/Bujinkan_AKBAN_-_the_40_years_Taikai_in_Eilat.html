Bujinkan AKBAN - the 40 years Taikai in Eilat

By Yossi Sheriff

40 years to Bujinkan Israel took place in Eilat Red Sea Honbu dojo at December from the 4th to the 6th, and focused on learning from all Bujinkan instructors in Israel. AKBAN veterans participated, and Yossi Sheriff, the AKBAN Academy headmaster and a certified Bujinkan Shidoshi, showed some of the traditional aspects of the advanced syllabus in AKBAN.
