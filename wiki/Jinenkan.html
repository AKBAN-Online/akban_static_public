Jinenkan

By Yossi Sheriff

Jissen Kobudo Jinenkan, (ג'יננקאן) literally "real fighting weapon arts of the natural way", is a martial arts school founded by Fumio Manaka ("Unsui") in 1996.

Jinenkan is based in different Japanese koryu, but mainly in those koryu originating from the Takamatzu den Ninjutsu.

The Jinenkan emphasizes Taijutsu (unarmed fighting skills) but also teaches Bikenjutsu (sword arts), Yari (spear), Naginata (halberd), Tessen (iron fan), Jutte (truncheon), Kusari-fundo (weighted chain), Tanto (knife), Bo, Hanbo, Jo (staff arts).
