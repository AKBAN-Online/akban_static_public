Description of the technique

Uke attacks with tsuki, tori stands in Shizen tai no kamae and performs tai sabaki in 45 degrees to the right and back while grabbing uke's right wrist with his right hand. Tori then pulls uke forward to the ground and locks uke's right arm with his knee on the elbow.

Video of the technique

This Kata is part of the Budo Ninjutsu Togakure Ryu Martial Arts.
