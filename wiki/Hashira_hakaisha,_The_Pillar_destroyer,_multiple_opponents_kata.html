Hashira hakaisha, The Pillar destroyer, multiple opponents kata

By Yossi Sheriff

Hashira Hakaisha, 柱破壊者, השירה הקאישה, is a multiple opponents Ninjutsu kata. It uses a sabaki and fudo ken to deliver strikes to several opponents while breaking through.
