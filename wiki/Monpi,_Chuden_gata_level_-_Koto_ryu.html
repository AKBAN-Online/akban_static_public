Monpi, (Kōtō), (捫飛), (מונפי נו קאטה - קוטו ריו - נינג'יטסו), (hands-on jump), is a Kata that is a part of the Koto Ryu koryu of Ninjutsu, It belongs to the third Chuden level.

Description of Monpi no kata

Walking towards the opponent tori grabs a pressure point at the armpit and pushes with boshi ken. After one step tori kicks uke at the front of the hip and toppels him down.
