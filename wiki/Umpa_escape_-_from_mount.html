Background of Umpa escape - from mount

This ground fighting technique is part of the syllabus of Brazilian Jiu Jitsu, Judo and Budo Ninjutsu. This is an escape from a Mount situation.

Description of Umpa escape - from mount

First of all, you grab with your hand his opposing side of the suit, near the neck, and with you second hand you grab the near hand. Than, place both feet on the ground as stable as possible, and use your stomach and the power from your legs to jump the enemy up, and roll him on the direction of his held hand.
