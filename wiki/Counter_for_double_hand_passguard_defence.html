Counter for double hand passguard defence

By Yossi Sheriff

Counter for double hand passguard defence, is a ground fighting technique, part of the syllabus of Brazilian Jiu Jitsu, Judo and Budo Ninjutsu.

A common defense against the Double underhook pass is to try to put buttery hooks back in. This technique is a counter to that defense.
