Description of Oni kudaki

Opponent grabs defender clothes just above the chest.

  1. Defender lay the back of his hand on opponent hand, while doing a "holding tea tray" shape with his arm. Defender moves his other hand under opponents hand in order to catch his own hand, while moving small half-step forward.
  2. Defender tight his grab while doing a 180° turn move with his body.
  3. Defender does another 180° turn move, to the opposite direction, while bending down his leg (the one that is closer to his opponent) so his knee would touch the ground.
