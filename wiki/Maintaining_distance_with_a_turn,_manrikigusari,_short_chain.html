Maintaining distance with a turn, manrikigusari, short chain

By Yossi Sheriff

Video of a turning jump with a short chain


Maintaining distance with a short chain using a turning jump (שמירת מרחק בסיבוב, שרשרת קרב קצרה) is short chain technique that uses the Manrikigusari, Kusarifundo combat chain, to hit from side while opening the gap between the opponents.
