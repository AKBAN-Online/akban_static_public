Video of Oikake dori

Oikake dori, 追掛捕, Catching and capturing, (אויקקה דורי) is a basic kata from the Takagi Yoshin Ryu Ninjutsu system.


Description of Oikake dori

uke: Walking tori: Right ushiro mune dori, right geri to left kobura and pull down, uke lands on back, right sokki ken pressure to kimon and zanshin.
