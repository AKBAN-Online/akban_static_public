Matsu Kaze Ryote, Shoden Gata level - Shinden Fudō Ryū Jūtaijutsu

Video of Matsu Kaze Ryote



Matsu Kaze Ryote, 松風両手 , two handed pine wind, מטסו קזה ריוטה, is a kata from the fighting system of Fudo Ryu. It originates at the part of Fudo Ryu that deals with grappling, the Fudō Ryū Jūtaijutsu and is at the basic level, the Shoden Gata.

This kata has been integrated into the Ninjutsu syllabus of the Takamatsu den.
