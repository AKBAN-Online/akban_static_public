AKBAN wiki:Substitution

By Yossi Sheriff

Substitution is the one-time, permanent copying of any content on AKBAN wiki. It differs from transclusion, which continually updates as the linked-to content changes.


To substitute, add subst: after the opening braces, as in {{subst:page name}}. For example: substitution {{subst:stub}} in contrast to transclusion {{stub}}.
