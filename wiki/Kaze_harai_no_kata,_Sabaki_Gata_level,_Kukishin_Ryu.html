Video of Kaze harai (Fuu Futsu) no kata - Kukishinden ryu


Kaze harai (風払), (Wind payment), (קזה הראי), also called in Jinenkan Fuu-futsu, is a Kata.

This form is part of the Kukishin ryu Dakentaijutsu (九鬼神流打拳体術) koryu, belonging to the Sabaki gata level.

Kaze haraii is a part of the syllabus of the Takamatsu den schools of Ninjutsu: Bujinkan, Jinenkan, AKBAN.
