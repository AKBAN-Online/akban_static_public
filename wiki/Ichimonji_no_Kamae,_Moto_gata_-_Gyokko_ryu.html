Ichimonji no kamae moto gata is a variation technique from the kamae of Ichimonji or Seigan no kamae in Ninjutsu

Description of the technique

uke attacks from Dai jodan no kamae, tori stands in kosei no kamae, steps back while taking his left leg up and then performs Tai sabaki to the left side and attacks.
