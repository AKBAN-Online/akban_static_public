Nichigeki, 日撃, solar strike, ניצ'יגקי, is a kata from the traditional martial arts Fudo ryu school

Description of Nichigeki

The opponent tries to throw using a Morote seoe nage. The defender lowers his weight and twists against the throw. The defender then uses his fingers in Goshi ken, turns and throws the opponent down.
