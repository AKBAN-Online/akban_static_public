Video of Karame dori

Karame dori, 搦捕, stopping capture, (קראמה דורי) is a basic kata from the Takagi Yoshin Ryu.

Description of Karame dori

Tori: Walk up to right keri to suigetsu, fall on head.
Uke: Seiza, step up right and back, right uke, left dori keri, half stand and pull down with left nage no kamae, hit right kobura (calf muscle).
