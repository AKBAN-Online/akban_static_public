21 January 2013, Aikido principles in Ninjutsu, Shizen Shigoku level, Shinden Fudo Ryu - Ninjutsu training lessons

By Yossi Sheriff

These are January the 21st Ninjutsu training videos and Ninjutsu techniques from the Ramat Hasharon dojo learned at January, 2013.

We trained in Shizen no Kata, one of the katas that constitute the higher level (Shizen Shigoku level) of Fudo Ryu Daken Taijutsu. Most of the henka (variations) explored are very Aikido like in movement but not in intention. The techniques retain the Ninjutsu-randori feeling of setting up the opponent.
