Description of Seion no kata - Kukishinden Ryu

Tori and uke stand facing each other in Shizentai, tori grips uke's lapel and right sleeve, uke grips tori in the same fashion.

Tori applies pressure to uke's neck Kyusho with boshi, moves forward to uke's right, kicks uke's right leg with a Side knee kick and performs Osoto gari on uke.
