Kouki, (Koki), (Kōki), (扣鬼), (Konoki), (קואוקי נו קאטה - קוטו ריו - נינג'יטסו), (punching demons), is a Kata that is a part of the Koto Ryu koryu of Ninjutsu, It belongs to the fourth Okuden level.

Description of Kouki (Koki) no kata

Walking toward the opponent Tori hits with Happa to the ears, jumps and kicks uke in the chest, toppling him backwards.
