Soku boku, Hekito gata level - Koto ryu

By Yossi Sheriff

Video of Sokuboku no kata



Sokuboku, (stop kick), (蹴朴), (סוקובוקו נו קאטה - קוטו ריו - נינג'יטסו), is a Kata that is a part of the Koto Ryu koryu of Ninjutsu, It belongs to the second Hekito level.
