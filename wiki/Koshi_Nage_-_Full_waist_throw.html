Koshi Nage - Full waist throw

By Yossi Sheriff

Koshi Nage (קושי נגה) is an Ninjutsu throw that lifts the opponent on his center of balance and rotates him on this axis.

Koshi nage is one of the Judo throws and Budo Ninjutsu throws that is classified as a hip throwing technique. Ninjutsu Koshi Nage is sometimes performed with a wide stance and the intention of throwing the opponent so a kick or stomp can be administered.
