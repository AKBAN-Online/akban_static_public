Roll

By Yossi Sheriff

Roll, (גלגול), (廻転), is a movement on the ground by rotation. The movement can be in any direction and it's main use in Martial arts and Ninjutsu in particular is transforming a possible trauma from hitting the ground into a tangential directional movement.
