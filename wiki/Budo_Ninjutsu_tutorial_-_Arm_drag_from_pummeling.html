Budo Ninjutsu tutorial - Arm drag from pummeling

By Yossi Sheriff

Pummeling is an important drill teaching advanced grappling skills that lead to throws and take downs. Using this drill we can implement passing the opponents' hand by using arm drag. Repeating this exercise many times creates an instinct that allows fast reaction and leads the fighter to taking the opponents back, an advantage position.
