Fudoza no Kamae - Ninjutsu

Za-Kamae Fudoza, 坐之構 不動坐, immovable sitted posture, זה קמאה פודוזה, is a combat posture, kamae that is used in Shinden Fudo ryu in the Takamatsu den.
