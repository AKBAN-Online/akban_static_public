Yoko Tomoe nage, Side sacrifice throw

By Yossi Sheriff

Yoko tomoe nage (יוקו טומואה נגה) or Yoko otoshi is a sacrifice throw with which one uses his own falling body weight to unbalance the opponent. The Yoko tomoe nage utilizes this while tripping the opponent to his side or, in another variation, straight forward while using a side fall.


This is a Judo throw that is classified as a sacrifice throwing technique.
