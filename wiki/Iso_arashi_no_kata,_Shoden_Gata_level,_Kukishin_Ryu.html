Iso Arashi (磯嵐), (Rocky shore storm), (איזו אראשי), is a Kata of the Kukishin ryu koryu, It belongs to the Shoden gata level in the Takamatsu den Ninjutsu schools.

Description of Iso Arrashi

Both in Shizen.

Uke grabs Troi's right lapel with left hand. Tori wraps his hand around uke's grip. Uke attacks with tsuki and a push kick. Tori block with a jodan yuke and a gedan yuke. Tori does a kuma te to uke's face and then throws him with an osoto gake.

Basic techniques used

  2. Tsuki,

  4. Jodan Yuke,
  5. Kuma te,
  6. Gedan Yuke,
  7. Osoto gake.
