Morote Seoi nage - Lifting shoulder throw

By Yossi Sheriff

Morote Seoi Nage, 背負い投げ, מורוטה סיאואה נגה, is a Judo throw that is classified as a hand throwing technique. Morote-Seoi-nage (doing Seoi-nage or Eri-seoi with two hands) is called Seoi-nage also.
