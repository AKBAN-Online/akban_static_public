Seoi

By Yossi Sheriff

Seoi, 背負い, (סאואה) (also, in mispronanciation, Seoe) is a Koryu throw involving an armlock on the opponent's elbow. Seoe is used in Ninjutsu. It is not legal in Judo.

It uses the same principle as Ippon seoe nage. The variation shown here is a counter to Tsuki.
