Nanme ushiro omote waki uchi - diagonally back and outside the attack

By Yossi Sheriff

Video of Nanme ushiro omote waki uchi


Nanme ushiro omote waki uchi is the name of a hanbo technique that emphasizes tai sabaki. It is part of the weapon syllabus of the Takamatsu den Ninjutsu.
