Kihon

By Yossi Sheriff

Kihon, (基本), (קיהון), literally the basics, is the name in many martial arts and in Ninjutsu of the fundamental techniques that are the building stones of the system. The kihon is exemplified by the Kihon Happo, a concise collection of important Ninjutsu techniques.
