Karai, 霞雷, fog thunder, קראי, is a kata that forms part of the Ten no kata level of Fudo Ryu Daken Tai Jutsu.



Description of Karai no Kata

Uke holds tori's lapel with the right hand, tori punches with the right hand to uke's stomach, grabs uke's right wrist and right shoulder then takes uke down to the ground on his stomach and applies pressure by taking uke's left and right arms and bringing them closer.
