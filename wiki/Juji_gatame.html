Juji gatame

By Yossi Sheriff

Juji-gatame (十字固) literally: "cross armlock" or "cross arm breaker" may refer to any of the following:

  • Juji gatame - Arm bar - from mount
  • Juji gatame - Arm bar - from the guard
  • Juji gatame - Arm bar - from knee on stomach
