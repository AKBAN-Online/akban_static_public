Yubi jime - Gi choke with little finger, is a choke on the blood vessels of the neck (blood choke hold) performed either standing or at ground fighting from mount or guard positions. This choke is a part of the syllabus of Brazilian Jiu Jitsu, Judo and Budo Ninjutsu.

Description of Yubi jime

Press with sides of the palms and the collar the carotid arteries.
