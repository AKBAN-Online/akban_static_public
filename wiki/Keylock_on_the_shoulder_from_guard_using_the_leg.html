Keylock on the shoulder from guard using the leg

By Yossi Sheriff

Video of Keylock on the shoulder - from the guard


Keylock on the shoulder from guard using the leg or oni kudaki is an armlock that dislocates the opponent's shoulder while being on the opponent's stomach crosswise. Keylock - from mount is a part of the syllabus of Brazilian Jiu Jitsu, Judo and Budo Ninjutsu.
