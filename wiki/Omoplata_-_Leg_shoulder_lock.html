Omoplata - Leg shoulder lock

By Yossi Sheriff

Video of Omoplata, Leg shoulder lock - from the guard


Omoplata - Leg shoulder lock is a strong technique meant to dislocate the shoulder and is done from the guard.

Omoplata is a part of the syllabus of Brazilian Jiu Jitsu, Judo and Budo Ninjutsu.
