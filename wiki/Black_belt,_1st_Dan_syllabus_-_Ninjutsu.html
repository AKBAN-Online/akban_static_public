Black belt, 1st Dan syllabus - Ninjutsu

By Yossi Sheriff

Black belt, 初段, Shodan, or 1st Dan, page lists all the video of the Ninjutsu techniques required in the AKBAN academy to pass graduation. This exam includes all the material from the previous belt exams.

A regular time-frame to gain the expertise for the Shodan test in AKBAN is about 12 years. The exam is conducted with the Headmaster and senior instructors. Any examiner can veto down the exam even if the majority ruled otherwise. In case of a veto the practitioner does not pass at that date.


The Black belt Belt exam (חגורה שחורה) marks the culmination of a long effort but does not necessarily marks the end of the practice.
