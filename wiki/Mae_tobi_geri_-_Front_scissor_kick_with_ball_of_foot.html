Mae tobi geri - Front scissor kick with ball of foot

By Yossi Sheriff

Front jumping scissor kick with ball of foot, Mae tobi geri, ( בעיטת מספריים קדמית אחרי בעיטת סטירה), is a Ninjutsu kick that uses the yoko aruki sabaki to close distance and attack.
