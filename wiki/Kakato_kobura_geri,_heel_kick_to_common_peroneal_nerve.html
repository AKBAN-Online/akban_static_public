Kakato kobura geri, heel kick to common peroneal nerve

By Yossi Sheriff

Heel kick to the common peroneal nerve, Kakato kobura geri (קקטו קובורה גרי), is a Ninjutsu kick to the back side of the fibular head.
The kick is used to disable an opponent by paralyzing the common pereoneal nerve.
