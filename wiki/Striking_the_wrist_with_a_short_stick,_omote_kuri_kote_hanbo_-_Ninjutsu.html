Striking the wrist with a short stick, omote kuri kote hanbo - Ninjutsu

By Yossi Sheriff

Video of rotational short stick strike to wrist


The Omote kuri kote hanbo (ימכת חיצונית בסיבוב לפרק כף יד יריב, אומוטה קורי קוטה, מקל קצר, נינג'יטסו) is a short stick (Hanbo) technique in Ninjutsu. The heavy weight of the traditional material of the Japanese short stick - Oak - makes one hand swings problematic because of the inertia of the stick.
