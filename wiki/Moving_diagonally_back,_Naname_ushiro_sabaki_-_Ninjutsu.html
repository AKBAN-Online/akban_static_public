Moving diagonally back, Naname ushiro sabaki - Ninjutsu

By Yossi Sheriff

Video of Naname ushiro sabaki - Moving backward diagonally


Naname ushiro sabaki (התחמקות באלכסון לאחור), evading an attack diagonally backward, is an important movement technique in Ninjutsu. This technique breaks the line of attack while at the same time creating maximal distance between the opponent and defender.
