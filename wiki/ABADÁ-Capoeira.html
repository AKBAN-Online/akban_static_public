ABADÁ-Capoeira

By Yossi Sheriff

Abada is a non-profit Capoeira educational organization that is centered in Brazil and has many faculties world wide. Abada Capoeira organization was founded by Mestre Camisa, José Tadeu Carneiro Cardoso.
