Back sliding step evasion, Ushiro sabaki - Ninjutsu

By Yossi Sheriff

Video of Ushiro Tai sabaki


Ushiro sabaki (אושירו סבאקי) is an evasion and movement technique in Ninjutsu. While creating distance from the opponent the body does not change front and rear legs.
