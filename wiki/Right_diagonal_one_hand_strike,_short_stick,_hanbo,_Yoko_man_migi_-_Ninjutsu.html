Right diagonal one hand strike, short stick, hanbo, Yoko man migi - Ninjutsu

By Yossi Sheriff

Video of striking diagonally from the right - short stick


The Yoko man migi (יוקו מן מיגי, הנבו) is a short stick (Hanbo) technique in Ninjutsu. The heavy weight of the traditional material of the Japanese short stick - Oak - makes one hand swings problematic because of the inertia of the stick.
