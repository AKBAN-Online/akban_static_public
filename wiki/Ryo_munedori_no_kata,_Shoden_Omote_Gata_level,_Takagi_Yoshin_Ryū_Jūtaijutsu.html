Video of Ryo munedori

Ryo mune dori, 両胸捕, double chest capture, (ריו מונה דורי) is a basic kata from the Takagi Yoshin Ryu system of Ninjutsu.


Description of Ryomune dori

tori: Ryote mune dori (right up, left lower). uke: Right cover right, step back left, swing right shuki ken down to take off left dori, left dori to right hijizume, left geri to right sai to take face down, retain dori to hijizume, zanshin.
