Video of Keylock - Oni kudaki - from side control


Keylock from side control or oni kudaki is an armlock that dislocates the opponent's shoulder while being on the opponent's stomach crosswise. Keylock - from mount is a part of the syllabus of Brazilian Jiu Jitsu, Judo and Budo Ninjutsu.
