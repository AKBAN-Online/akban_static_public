Teki gaeshi, (Tekigaeshi), (擲返), (טקי גאשי נו קאטה - קוטו ריו - נינג'יטסו), (throw back), is a Kata that is a part of the Koto Ryu koryu of Ninjutsu, It belongs to the third Chuden level.

Description of Teki gaeshi

uke attacks with tsuki from seigan no kamae, tori from shizen tai no kamae performs tai sabaki to the right and back while catching uke's right arm under his left arm. Tori then pushes uke's pelvis down, pushing uke's right knee with his right leg and then kicks the the left knee to take uke to the ground.
