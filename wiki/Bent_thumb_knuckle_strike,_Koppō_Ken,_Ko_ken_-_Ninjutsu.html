By Yossi Sheriff

Video of hold release using koppo ken


Koppo ken, Ko ken, 骨法拳 (קופו קן), is striking form in takamatzu den basic Ninjutsu hand strikes. This punch uses the bent knucle of the thumb to deliver short range, rotational, strikes to pressure points.
