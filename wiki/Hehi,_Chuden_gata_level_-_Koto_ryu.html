Hehi, (ההי נו קאטה - קוטו ריו - נינג'יטסו), is a Kata that is a part of the Koto Ryu koryu of Ninjutsu, It belongs to the third Chuden level.

Description of Hehi no kata

Against a walking Uke, in walking stride, one hits the opponent's neck pressure point with a Boshi ken, Shikan ken or a Goshin tsuki in one hand and afterwards throws the opponent on the ground with the other hand striking in Kuma te .
