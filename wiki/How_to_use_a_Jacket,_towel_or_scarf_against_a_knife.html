How to use a Jacket, towel or scarf against a knife

By Yossi Sheriff

Using a scarf, a Jacket or a Cape in self defense is a summery of Ninjutsu techniques that was learned in Sukkot 2014 weapon seminar in AKBAN. These techniques utilize a cape, a Jacket or a towel to trap, ensnare or deceive the opponent who attacks with a Kunai.
