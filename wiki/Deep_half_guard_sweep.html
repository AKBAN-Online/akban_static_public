Deep half guard sweep

By Yossi Sheriff

Video of Deep half guard sweep


Deep half guard sweep is a basic sweep from the guard aimed at pushing the opponent on his side and obtaining [[::Category:Attack from Half guard|Half guard]]. It can also be used as a set-up for many other techniques. This technique forms a part of the syllabus of Brazilian Jiu Jitsu, Judo and Budo Ninjutsu.
