Kyo e no kamae (קיו אה, סכין) is a Ninjutsu knife stance that hides the Tento and Kunai knives. In this stance the hand holding the knife is hidden behind one's back. the other hand points to the ground in a manner the opens up the stance to all manner of cuts.
