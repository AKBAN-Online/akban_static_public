Mae sokuto geri, front hip joint kick

By Yossi Sheriff

Mae sokuto geri, Kinketsu geri, Front hip joint kick, front knee joint kick (בעיטה קדמית לברך), is a Ninjutsu push kick to the area in front of the opponent's hip joint.


The purpose of this kick is to unbalance the opponent by forcing him to bend forward.
This kick is used when grappling and also in striking distance. It is relatively harmless.
