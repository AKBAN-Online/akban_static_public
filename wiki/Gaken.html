Gaken

From shizen tai uke Attacks with tsuki, tori blocks with jodan yuke, grabs uke's right wrist and right shoulder and kicks uke's stomach with mae geri then takes down uke on his stomach and applies presure on the elbow of the right hand by taking the left hand by bringing it closer to the right hand.

This Kata is a part of the Ninjutsu Fudo Ryu Martial Arts
