Tangeki, Tan-geki, (擔撃), (carry and punch), (טנגקי נו קאטה - קוטו ריו - נינג'יטסו), is a Kata that is a part of the Koto Ryu koryu of Ninjutsu, It belongs to the basic Shoden level.

Description of Tangeki no kata

From Hoko no kamae. Tori then attacks with a left push kick to the sternum and (henka) and a double Tsuki to the stomach while going down on one knee.
