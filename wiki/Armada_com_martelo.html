Armada com martelo, is also called Parfuzo, a screw, which describes the aerial movement of the body. The move is a jumping, spinning Capoeira kick combining the Armada kick with the Martelo.

Description of Armada com martelo

The capoeirista starts with the same move of the Armada spining kick, before the foot gets to the ground he raises up the other leg sending a roundhouse Martelo kick. The torso is parallel to the floor.
