Video of Kaigo Kudaki

Kaigo kudaki, 戒後砕, Crushing a rear attack, (קייגו קודאקי) is a basic kata from the Takagi Yoshin Ryu.

Description of Kaygo kudaki

Uke walks behind tori and tries to get a nelson lock, tori grabs uke's right wrist and does a duck under the right arm to the back and the right, punches uke in the chin, grabs the right shoulder and takes uke to the ground.
Uke does Zenpo kaiten, front roll, and stays on his back, tori locks the right arm with his leg on uke's ribs.
