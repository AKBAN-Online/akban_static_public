Yoko man kuki kusari - Hitting both temples, Short chain

By Yossi Sheriff

Video of Hitting both temples with a short chain


This short chain striking technique (יוקו מן קוקי קוסארי, מכות לשתי הרקות, שרשרת קרב קצרה) is a Ninjutsu technique from the AKBAN syllabus, uses the Kusarifundo combat chain, the Manrikigusari to hit the head from side.
