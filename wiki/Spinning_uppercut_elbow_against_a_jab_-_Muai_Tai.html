Spinning uppercut elbow against a jab - Muai Tai

By Yossi Sheriff

Summary of Spinning uppercut elbow against a jab

This is a Muay Tai technique that is classified as an elbow technique.

Description of Spinning uppercut elbow against a jab

As the opponent jabs; block his jab with your back hand, follow up immediately with a cross elbow with the same hand to his face. Turn counter clockwise (if you started standing with your left foot in front), and deliver an upward moving back elbow with your previously front hand (left elbow) to his chin.
