Jumonji no kamae (ג'ומונג'י, סכין) is a Ninjutsu knife stance that uses the Tento and Kunai knives. In this stance the hand holding the knife is crossed in front of the body. In the reverse grip the knife either points forward or is hidden behind the forarm.

This is similar to the Ninjutsu kamae - Jumonji.
