Portal: Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu, BJJ

By Yossi Sheriff

The BJJ portal in the AKBAN wiki is a testament to the importance of the techniques and perspectives that Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu practitioners have been developing.

Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu is a mainstay in the syllabus of AKBAN with an ongoing process of learning that we do with the help of BJJ teachers.

I am Yossi Sheriff, the AKBAN academy headmaster, and I am leading a decades long project of filming and documenting martial arts that intersect with my perspective of Ninjutsu. This is a grassroots project, with no business model or ads to finance it. Any criticism is welcomed. Please mail us or use our twitter, facebook or youtube to suggest techniques and perspectives
