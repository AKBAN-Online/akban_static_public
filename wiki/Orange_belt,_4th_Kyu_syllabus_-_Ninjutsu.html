Orange belt grade, 四級, in Ninjutsu (4th kyu) lists the Ninjutsu techniques, martial and physical requirments in the Akban Academy. The Orange belt (חגורה כתומה), test prerequisites are at least two years in the dojo.

The tests in Akban form an important point in the maturation process of the martial arts trainees in Akban.
