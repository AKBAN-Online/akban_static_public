Video of Iki Chigai (Yuki chigai)

Yuki chigai, 行違, or Ikki Chigai, Line breach, (יוקי צ'יגאי) is a basic kata from the Takagi Yoshin Ryu.

Description of Yuki (Iki) chigai

Uke: Walking passed on right. Tori: As A's right arm swings forward right dori and lift up, left shuki ken to right wakitsubo, move back right to take A's right foot, will fall on back, keep dori, Push kick to butsumetsu Kyusho.
