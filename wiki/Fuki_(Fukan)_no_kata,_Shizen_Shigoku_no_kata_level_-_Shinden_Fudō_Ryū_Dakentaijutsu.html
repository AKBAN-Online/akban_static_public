Fuki or Fukan, 不諱, without concealing, פוקי (פוקאן), is a kata that forms part of the Shizen Shigoku level of Fudo Ryu Daken Tai Jutsu learned as a part of the Ninjutsu syllabus in Jinenkan, Bujinkan and AKBAN.

Fukan Kata description

Uke grabs tori and tries to throw tori with morote seoe nage, tori moves to uke's right after lowering his abdomen. Tori then releases his right arm and hits uke's face with a right punch and uke's liver with a left punch, grabs uke's waist and throws uke with yoko sutemi. Tori finishes by hitting uke with an ura ken while on the ground.
