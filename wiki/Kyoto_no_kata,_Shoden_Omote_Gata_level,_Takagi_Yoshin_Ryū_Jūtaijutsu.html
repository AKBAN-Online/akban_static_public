Video of Kyoto no kata

Kyotō no kata, 虚倒, empty collapse, (קיוטו נו קאטה) is a basic kata from the Takagi Yoshin Ryu.

Description of Kyoto no kata

Uke is in front of tori, sitting in Seiza. Tori attacks in right tsuki. Uke does naname mae sabaki and grabs right hand of tori with his right hand and blocks tori's right shoulder with his left shoulder.

Uke kicks Mae geri to opponent's heart region, pulls tori on his face and pins him down with Musha dori.
