Hyoho Niten Ichi-ryu

By Yossi Sheriff

Hyōhō Niten Ichi-ryū is a koryu Martial system originated in Japan. This mainly sword fighting system was alegedly based on the teaching of the legendary swordsman Myamoto Musashi.

Origin

At aproximately 1640, Musashi intended to pass his Menkyo Kaiden to Terao Magonojo, his younger brother Kyumanosuke, and to Furuhashi Sozaemon.

Succession

Terao Kyumanosuke (Motomenosuke) had received the Menkyo kaiden of the Niten ichi Ryu.
