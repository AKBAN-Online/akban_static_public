Hon jime - Trachea choke, standing from behind

By Yossi Sheriff

Video closeup of hon jime - trachea choke


Hon jime - trachea choke, (הון ג'ימה) is a choke on the airway performed either standing or in sitting position. This choke is a part of the syllabus of Judo and Ninjutsu.
