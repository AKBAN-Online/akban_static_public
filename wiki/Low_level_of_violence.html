Low level of violence

By Yossi Sheriff

Low level of violence denotes a technique that initially causes a little harm and is controllable.

Low level of violence is an attribute of a meta perspective on the AKBAN wiki that is a derivative of Level of violence, a term used in AKBAN to divide techniques by their results rather then by their constituents.
