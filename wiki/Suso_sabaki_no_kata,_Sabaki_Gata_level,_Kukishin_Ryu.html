Video of Suso sabaki no kata - Kukishinden Ryu



Suso sabaki (裾捌), (Grabbing the garment), (סוסו סבאקי), called in Jinenkan Sode sabaki (袖捌), is a Kata.

This form is part of the Kukishin ryu Dakentaijutsu (九鬼神流打拳体術) koryu, belonging to the Sabaki gata level.

Suso sabaki is a part of the syllabus of the Takamatsu den schools of Ninjutsu: Bujinkan, Jinenkan, AKBAN.
