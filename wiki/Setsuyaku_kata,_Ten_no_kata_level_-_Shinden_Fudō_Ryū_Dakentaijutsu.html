Setsuyaku, 雪耀, snow sparkle, סטסויאקו, is a kata that forms part of the Ten no kata level of Fudo Ryu Daken Tai Jutsu.


Description of Setsuyaku

Uke attacks with a Tsuki. Tori blocks with Jodan Yuke and grabs the hand from the inside. Tori then performs a low Te makura and throws with Ippon Seoi Nage on uke's side.
