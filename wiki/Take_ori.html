Description of Take ori

uke and tori in shizen tai, uke holds tori's shirt with his left hand, tori grabs uke's wrist with his right hand on top of uke's hand then takes the arm down and up while applying presure on the wrist by pushing uke's elbow towards the wrist with his left hand.
