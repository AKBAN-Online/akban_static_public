Blue belt grade syllabus, 2nd Kyu, 二級, in Ninjutsu is a comprehensive source of video and articles to prepare an AKBAN student in the Ninjutsu techniques that are required in the exam.

The Blue belt (2nd Kyu) exam in AKBAN sums an important point in the years of learning toward the Black belt exam - It emphasizes Striking, Kicking, throwing and Joint lock skills. The Average time for learning for the Blue belt exams is 5-7 years dojo time. The Blue belt (חגורה כחולה), is an essential 'two thirds of the way' exam that emphasizes explosive strikes and movements.
