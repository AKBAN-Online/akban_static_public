Kata maki (片巻), (קטה מקי נו קאטה - קוטו ריו - נינג'יטסו), (Entangling one side) is a kata of the Koto Ryu koryu in the Takamatsu Den schools of Ninjutsu, It belongs to the basic Shoden level.

Description of Kata maki no kata

Uke attacks with right Tsuki and left Tsuki, tori blocks accordingly with Jodan yuke to both Tsuki then grabs the left hand and performs Gosha dori then takes uke down with Boshi ken to the neck.
