Totoku Hyoshi no kamae


Tōtoku Hyōshi no kamae (刀匿礮姿), (טוטוקו היושי) is a kneeling stance, kamae, that originated in Togakure ryu - Ninjutsu and is practiced in the martial system of Takamazu den Koryu.

Totoku Hyoshi no kamae is an important element in Ninjutsu Sabaki as it comprises the intermediate phase between being prostrate and erect.
