Description of Fubuki kata

Both in Shizen tai no kamae Uke grabs tori's lapel with left hand. Tori wraps his hand around Uke's left hand. Uke attacks with right Tsuki and a right push kick. Tori blocks the tsuki with jodan yuke and the kick with gedan yuke while doing yoko tai sabaki. Tori kicks to the groin with left leg, grabs uke's left elbow, and does a modified musha dori lock throwing uke on his back.
