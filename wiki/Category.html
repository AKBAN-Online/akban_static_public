Category

By Yossi Sheriff

Category, in the Akban-wiki is a way to gather related martial information and connect it to other related articles.

One can think of a category as a virtual shelf in a library. By putting Category:kicks inside brackets [[Category:kicks]] the edited article or technique resides virtually inside the kick - category. This semantic feature enables a reader to browse to related articles via a tree like structure:

An article can belong to multiple categories there by facilitating interrelated articles. Categories can reside inside other categories and form a hierarchy.
