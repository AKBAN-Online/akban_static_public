Shoten tombogaeri - Back somersault from wall running

By Yossi Sheriff

Video of Back somersault from wall running


Back somersault from wall running, Shoten, (סלטה אחורית לאחר טיפוס על קיר), is a rarely used Ninjutsu technique that necessitates the ability to do back somersault.

Description of Back somersault from wall running

Gaining speed perform only one or two steps on the wall and let your feet climb higher then your torso. Use this momentum to do a back somersault and return to shizen tai. Stepping toward the wall should be in a controlled and accurate manner. This technique is extremely dangerous if done without the proper instruction.
