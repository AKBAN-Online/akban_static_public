24th December 2012, Learning the basic Ninjutsu rolls - Ninjutsu training lessons

By Yossi Sheriff

Use this page to be up to date with current Ninjutsu sessions in the dojo - Keep training!.

These are December the 24 Ninjutsu training videos and Ninjutsu techniques from the Ramat Hasharon dojo learned at December 2012.

A gradual approach to the basic Ninjutsu rolls allowed us to practice correct rolls on a hard surface. The rolls learned were: Forward Ninjutsu roll, Backward Ninjutsu roll, Side roll, Diagonal roll and rolling from Seigan no Kamae.
