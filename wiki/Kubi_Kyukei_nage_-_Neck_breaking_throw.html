Kubi Kyukei nage - Neck breaking throw

By Yossi Sheriff

Kubi kyukei nage, (首休憩), (קובי קיוקאי נגה) is a dangerous Ninjutsu throw that can break the opponent's neck in a mechanism similar to hanging.
