Hosoku (捕捉), (הוסוקו נו קאטה - קוטו ריו - נינג'יטסו), (Capture) is a kata of the Koto Ryu koryu in the Takamatsu Den schools of Ninjutsu, It belongs to the Shoden level.

Description of Hosoku no kata

Uke attacks with a right Tsuki followed by a left Tsuki. Tori blocks both attacks with Jodan yuke, attacks with a right Boshi ken to the point where the right thigh meets the stomach, and finishes with a headbutt.
