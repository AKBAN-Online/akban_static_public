Magelan bag

By Yossi Sheriff

Magelan bag (שק מגלן) is a training weight device that was developed by Lior Katz, a certified physical therapist and an Akban veteran.
The Magelan bag is a low cost solution for functional core body fitness that utilizes exercises that work on many muscle groups. It is made from an inner tube filled with sand or iron powder.
