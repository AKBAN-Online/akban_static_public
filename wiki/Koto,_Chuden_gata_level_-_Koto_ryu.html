Koto, (Kōtō), (喉倒), (קוטו נו קאטה - קוטו ריו - נינג'יטסו), (throat debt), is a Kata that is a part of the Koto Ryu koryu of Ninjutsu, It belongs to the third Chuden level.

Description of koto no kata

Tori attacks with Sanshin ken and Kin geri and then does Naname ushiro sabaki - Moving backward diagonally.

Henka (varietion) of koto no kata

Uke attacks tori with tsuki, tori steps forward and to the right while holding uke's right arm with his left arm and uke's right shoulder with his right arm. Tori then attacks with Mae geri and boshi ken to the neck.
