Chikusei (竹声), (Bamboo voice), (צ'יקוסיי), is a Kata.

This form is part of the Kukishin ryu Dakentaijutsu (九鬼神流打拳体術) koryu, belonging to the Sabaki gata level.

Chikusei is a part of the syllabus of the Takamatsu den schools of Ninjutsu: Bujinkan, Jinenkan, AKBAN.
