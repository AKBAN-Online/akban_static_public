Keylock - Oni kudaki - from mount

By Yossi Sheriff

Video of Keylock - Oni kudaki - from mount


Keylock from mount or oni kudaki is an armlock that dislocates the opponent's shoulder while sitting on the opponent's stomach. Keylock - from mount is a part of the syllabus of Brazilian Jiu Jitsu, Judo and Budo Ninjutsu.
