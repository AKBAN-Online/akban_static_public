Beginner level of expertise

By Yossi Sheriff

Beginner level of expertise is the elementary level of techniques and some basic kata.

This level is the stepping stone to the ken of martial arts and includes the physical ability to execute effective and simple techniques.

Beginner level of expertise is an attribute of the meta perspective - "level of expertise", one of the terms in the AKBAN methodical system, the methodical pyramid.
