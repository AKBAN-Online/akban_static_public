Meta perspectives in martial arts

By Yossi Sheriff

Meta perspectives in martial arts is a Methodical Pyramid term used in AKBAN to mark constituents of techniques other then its physical and anatomical aspects.

The meta perspectives of the techniques semantically written in so far are Levels of expertise, timing and level of violence.
