Ichimonji no kamae (איצ'י מונג'י נו קמאה - עמידה ראשונה), is a combat stance in Ninjutsu. This stance is the precursor of many kata in Takamatsu den Ninjutsu. Seigan no kamae is called Ichimonji in some schools.
