Juji gatame from mount is an armbar intended to break the elbow on the ground. Juji gatame is a part of the syllabus of Brazilian Jiu Jitsu, Judo and Budo Ninjutsu.

  • For other versions of Juji gatame

Description of Juji gatame - Arm bar - from mount

The attacker grabs the wrist of the opponent. One of the attacker's legs goes across the opponent's chest, The other on the opponent's face or neck. The arm is being held between the thighs, with the elbow pointing against the thigh or hips. By clinching the opponent's wrist to the attacker's chest, the attacker can hyper extend the opponent's arm and break the elbow. The attacker can apply pressure on the elbow joint by arching the hips.
