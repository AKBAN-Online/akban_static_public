Video of Hiza guruma (Sono Ichi)

Hiza guruma 膝車, knee wheel, (היזה גורומה) is a basic level kata from the Takagi Yoshin Ryu.

Description of Hiza guruma (Sono Ichi)

  • Uke: Sits in seiza, right tsuki to suigetsu Kyusho.
  • Tori: Walk up, right hit tsuki and kote dori, stand on right side, left dori to right daimon, stretch out arm and right throw, twist and push up to right arm with left ken.
