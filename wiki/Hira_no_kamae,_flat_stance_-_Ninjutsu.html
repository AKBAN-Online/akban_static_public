Hira no kamae (הירה נו קמאה), (平之構), (first flat posture), is a Ninjutsu stance that is used in Kukishin Ryu. Attention, This stance has a similar name to Hira ichimonji no kamae but different uses and characteristics.

Description of Hira ichimonji no kamae

In this posture, both legs are holding the body's weight equally. The legs are posted at the width of the shoulders, knees slightly bent. Both palms are lightly put over the hip fold.
