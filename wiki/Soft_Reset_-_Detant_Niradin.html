Detant Background

AKBAN Detant (דטאנט) teaches emotion regulation, mindfulness and resilience mainly through physical micro procedures.

Stress response during confrontation blocks the procedural memory and leads to hormonal, neural and cognitive phenomena that narrow our field of vision, impair judgment and fine motor skills, and lead to a rapid escalation of the conflict. Yossi Sheriff, the AKBAN Academy headmaster, developed Detant syllabus for extreme professions and elite security units that need to maintain a high level of awareness while maintaining ethical agency in system two thinking.
