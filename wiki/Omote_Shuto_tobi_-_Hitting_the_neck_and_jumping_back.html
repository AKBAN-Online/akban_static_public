Omote Shuto tobi - Hitting the neck and jumping back

By Yossi Sheriff

Omote Shuto and tobi are a Ninjutsu technique aimed at the opponent's head or neck. After hitting the head or the neck with the side of his hand the attacker jumps diagonally backwards.
