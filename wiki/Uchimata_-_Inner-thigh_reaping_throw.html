Uchimata - Inner-thigh reaping throw

By Yossi Sheriff

Uchimata (אוצ'ימטה), is a Judo throw that is classified as a foot throwing technique. This techniques uses the same principles as Harai goshi - the opponent's center of gravity rotates on the practitioner's hip.
