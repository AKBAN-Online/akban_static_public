Budo Ninjutsu tutorial - Movement, Tai Sabaki

By Yossi Sheriff

Video of Budo Ninjutsu tutorial - Movement, Tai Sabaki


'Tai sabaki, when done outdoors should adapt to surface irregularities. When evading or attacking an opponent, one should use a sensitive stepping method.

The leading leg always steps from above and never trails or slides. The leg that follows can slide or jump too. When jumping the legs should land from above, again, never sliding on the surface.


This stepping method applies both to attacking and evasive movements, with or without weapons.
