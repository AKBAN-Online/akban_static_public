Yakov Schacht October 2015 seminar in AKBAN

By Yossi Sheriff

The Yakov Schacht October 2015 Ninjutsu seminar took place in AKBAN Tel Aviv dojo. The teacher was Yakov Schacht Sensei, the Hamburg Ninjutsu Academy headmaster.

The first part of Yakov Schacht Sensei's seminar was about Te Hodoki and ground fighting. The second part, that took place in Akban Ramat Hasharon, emphasized Koku no Kata.
