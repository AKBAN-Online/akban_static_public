Gendai budo

By Yossi Sheriff

Gendai budo, (現代武道), literally "modern martial ways", denotes the Japanese martial arts that were genrated and developed after the Meiji restoration (starting from 1866).

All Gendai budo systems originated in Japanese Koryu.
