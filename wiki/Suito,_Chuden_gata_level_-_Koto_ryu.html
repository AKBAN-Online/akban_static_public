Suito, (Suitō), (夊倒), (Suī to) (סויטו נו קאטה - קוטו ריו - נינג'יטסו), (extra fall), is a Kata that is a part of the Koto Ryu koryu of Ninjutsu, It belongs to the third Chuden level.

Description of Suito no kata

Uke attacks with right and left tsuki, tori blocks accordingly with jodan yuke then uke tries to throw tori in kubi nage. Tori moves with the throw and hit fudo ken to the chest and face. Tori then drops uke with a joint lock.
