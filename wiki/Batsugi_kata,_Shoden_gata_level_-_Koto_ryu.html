Batsugi no kata (拔技), (pulling abilty), (בטסוגי נו קאטה - קוטו ריו - נינג'יטסו), is part of the Koto Ryu koryu of Ninjutsu, It belongs to the basic Shoden level.

Description of Batsugi no kata

Uke holds tori's lapel with his right hand, tori then performs Omote gyaku while simultaneously attacking with right hand fingers to uke's eyes. Tori then sits on his left knee and rolls uke on his back.
