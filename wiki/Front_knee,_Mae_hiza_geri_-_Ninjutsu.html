Front knee, Mae hiza geri - Ninjutsu

By Yossi Sheriff

Front knee (ברכייה קדמית) is a short range kick that is used in many martial arts, Ninjutsu included.

Video of front knee


Description

While gathering momentum the trainee pushes the knee in the direction of the opponent using his hips, sometimes a pull with the hands or a jump.
