Kata guruma, Ninjutsu - Shoulder wheel

By Yossi Sheriff

Kata guruma, shoulder wheel or fireman's carry (קטה גורומה) is a hand throwing technique of Ninjutsu. The principle in most variations of the throw is blocking a hand and leg on the same side while unbalancing the opponent.
