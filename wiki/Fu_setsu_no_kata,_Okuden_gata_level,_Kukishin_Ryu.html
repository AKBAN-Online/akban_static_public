Fu setsu (風雪), (Wind and snow), also named in Bujinkan - Fūun (風雲), (Wind and clouds), (פוסטסו), is a Kata.

This combat - form is a part of the Kukishin ryu Dakentaijutsu (九鬼神流打拳体術) koryu, belonging to the lower advanced level Okuden gata.

Fusetsu is a part of the syllabus of the Takamatsu den schools of Ninjutsu: Bujinkan, Jinenkan, AKBAN.
