Ku no kata, Gyokko Ryū - Ninjutsu

By Yossi Sheriff

Video of Ku no kata


Ku no kata (風の型), (קו נו קאטה), is an important kata, one that belongs to the Go-gyo no kata (Go-gyo no kata) level, a level that is also called Sanshin no Kata (三心の型) in Gyokko Ryu Kosshijutsu. A part of the Takamatsu den Ninjutsu.


Description of Ku no kata

Uke does a tsuki attack to tori. tori evades back in a sabaki at 45 degree angle, abruptly putting his hand in front of the attacker and does a push kick with the heel.
