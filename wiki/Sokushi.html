Sokushi

By Yossi Sheriff

Sokushi (足指), or sokshi (סוקשי) is a Ninjutsu term for kicking with the tips of the toes.
Forming the toes of the foot into a thrusting point is difficult process but its upside is the ability to concentrate the force of many kicks to a tiny striking point.
