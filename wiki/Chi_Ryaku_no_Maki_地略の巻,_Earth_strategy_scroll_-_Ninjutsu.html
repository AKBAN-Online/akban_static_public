Chi Ryaku no Maki 地略の巻, Earth strategy scroll - Ninjutsu

By Yossi Sheriff

Chi Ryaku no Maki, 天略の巻, the Earth strategy scroll, is the name of the second part of a compilation of Ninjutsu techniques augmented in Bujinkan to facilitate ordered training in Ninjutsu.

Hajutsu Kyūhō, 破術九法, The nine methods of defeating techniques

  • Te Hodoki, 手解 – hand release
  • Tai Hodoki, 体解 – Body escape
  • Oya Goroshi, 親殺 – Thumb (big one) break
  • Ko Goroshi, 子殺 – Break the little finger (child)
  • Koshi Kudaki, 腰砕 – hip break
  • Happō Keri, 八方蹴 – Eight-directional kicking
  • Keri Kudaki, 蹴砕 – Kick breaker
  • Ken Kudaki, 拳砕 - Fist crusher
  • Henka Kudaki, 変化砕 – variation on fist crusher
