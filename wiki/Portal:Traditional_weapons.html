Portal:Traditional weapons

By Yossi Sheriff

Portal Traditional weapons, 従来の武器ポータル, lists the martial arts weapon techniques documented on the AKBAN wiki.

I am Yossi Sheriff, the AKBAN academy headmaster, and I am leading a decades long project that started in 1992, of filming and documenting martial arts. This is a grassroots project, with no business model or ads to finance it. Any criticism is welcomed. Please mail me or use our twitter, facebook or youtube to suggest techniques and perspectives
