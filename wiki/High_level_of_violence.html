High level of violence

By Yossi Sheriff

High level of violence is an attribute of a meta perspective on the AKBAN wiki that denotes a technique that initially causes severe harm and might be uncontrollable.

Level of violence is a meta perspective used in AKBAN to tag techniques by their results rather then by their constituents.
