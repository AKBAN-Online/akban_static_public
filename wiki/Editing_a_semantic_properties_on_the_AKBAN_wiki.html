Editing a semantic properties on the AKBAN wiki

By Yossi Sheriff

Pages and articles on the AKBAN wiki can get semantic markup. This markup consists of several attributes and parameters.

The kata pages get these meta perspectives:

  1. Timing
  2. Level of violence
  3. Strategy deployed

These meta perspectives have attributes:

  1. Timing - 1st Timing, 2nd Timing, 3rd Timing
  2. Level of violence - Low level of violence, Medium level of violence, High level of violence
  3. Strategy deployed

Techniques markup

Each kata uses basic techniques. The parameter is Uses the techniques it is written like this

===Basic techniques used=== This kata uses the following techniques: [[Uses the techniques::Tsuki]], [[Uses the techniques::Push kick]], [[Uses the techniques::Jodan Yuke]], [[Uses the techniques::Kuma te]], [[Uses the techniques::Gedan Yuke]], [[Uses the techniques::Osoto gake]].
