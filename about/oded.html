Oded Levy - AKBAN

Email: odedenator@gmail.com

Oded grew up in Jerusalem, Israel, and started studying Budo Ninjutsu in AKBAN under Yossi Sheriff in the end of the 90's.
He passed his black belt exam in 2010.

Oded has trained in Shotokan Karate, Judo, "Dennis Survival" and Ju Jitsu. Oded learned Krav Maga under Itay Gil after his combat service in the Israeli army infantry.
Oded has worked in security in high-risk locations around Jerusalem and had extensive gun and counter-terrorism training during his work.

After studying animation at the "Bezalel Academy of Art and Design" in Jerusalem, Oded worked as an art director in Tel Aviv and had his art exhibited in a show in Toronto, Canada, where he now resides with his cute wife.
Besides art, Oded's interests include astronomy, philosophy, hiking, and the Scottish Highlands - where he once backpacked through.
