Martial research


We are steadfast protectors of both law and tradition, ensuring thorough scrutiny of techniques and motivations.
Our headmaster at AKBAN Academy, Yossi Sheriff, has dedicated years to research, resulting in the creation of Detant - a practical emotion regulation system designed for professionals. This unique program focuses on teaching emotional regulation through bodily control. Concurrently, Sheriff has been using a systematic method for defense and martial arts research, known as 'The Methodical Pyramid'.
Our expansive database, referred to as the AKBAN wiki, employs semantic queries to categorize each move based on its level of violence, damage points, and counter-strategies. We acknowledge that every technique can be challenged and therefore maintain an open-minded approach. Our training integrates elements from western martial arts, including knife and weapon handling, with an emphasis on ground-work and MMA skills. This approach extends to modern weapons and training methodologies.
