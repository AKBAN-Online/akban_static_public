"Ninjas, renowned for their strategic thinking and innovative use of technology, serve as our inspiration in crafting a comprehensive and engaging online repository dedicated to martial arts. By leveraging technology and logic, we aim to enhance our teaching methods and effectively document the rich tapestry of martial culture.
Since our journey began in 1997, we've cultivated one of the internet's most extensive martial arts databases. Our site boasts a repository of over 1500 self-recorded technique videos and thousands of articles available in Japanese, English, and Hebrew.
While our initial focus lay in cataloging various martial arts, we've since expanded our scope to encompass more than just Ninjutsu. Our use of, initially, the semantic Wikimedia platform and now AI, enables us to structure and make sense of the vast database we've built.
