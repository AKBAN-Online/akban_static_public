Tradition


AKBAN Ninjutsu encompasses the ancient martial arts insights that originated centuries ago. Through the tireless dedication of successive generations of Japanese teachers, this invaluable knowledge has been preserved, ultimately flourishing in the Bujinkan under the esteemed leadership of Hatsumi Masaaki, the soke.
In Israel, where Doron Navon, the first non-Japanese Bujinkan Shihan, blazed the trail, we are proud to have nurtured four generations of students.
The richness of our tradition is meticulously upheld, as we commit ourselves to the repetition of ancient katas, etching them into our memories. This sacred knowledge finds permanence through our AKBAN wiki, a freely accessible repository of text and video documentation. Tradition and respect are inseparable companions on our journey, as we honor not only our own heritage but also the diverse martial traditions that enrich our collective understanding. Embodying this respect, we embrace the broader martial arts community, inviting masters from all disciplines to share their insights and learn alongside us.
